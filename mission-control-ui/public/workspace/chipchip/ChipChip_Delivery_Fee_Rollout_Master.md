# ChipChip Delivery Fee Rollout Strategy (Option B)

## 1. Customer Distribution & Rollout Strategy
To minimize churn and manage customer sentiment, the 3 Birr Home Delivery Fee and Free Super Leader Pickup option will be rolled out in three distinct phases based on customer value and purchase frequency.

*   **Phase 1: New & Low-Frequency Users (Weeks 1-2)**
    *   *Target:* Users with < 2 orders in the last 3 months.
    *   *Rationale:* Lowest risk of churn; these users have not yet formed rigid habits around free home delivery. 
*   **Phase 2: Mid-Tier Core Users (Weeks 3-4)**
    *   *Target:* Regular users (2-5 orders/month).
    *   *Rationale:* Test the operational capacity of the Super Leader Pickup network before the highest volume hits.
*   **Phase 3: High-Value "VIP" Customers (Weeks 5-6)**
    *   *Target:* Top 10% of users by Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) or > 5 orders/month.
    *   *Rationale:* Give our best customers the longest lead time, the softest landing, and exclusive transition perks.

## 2. Staggered Announcement Plan
Messaging will always lead with the *value* of the new feature (Free Super Leader Pickup) rather than the penalty (Home Delivery Fee).

*   **T-14 Days (The Teaser - Positive Framing):** 
    *   *Message:* "More ways to get your ChipChip orders! Introducing Free Super Leader Pickup in your neighborhood." (No mention of the fee yet).
*   **T-7 Days (The Policy Update - Transparent Framing):**
    *   *Message:* "Important updates to our delivery options." Explain that to maintain service quality and support drivers, a nominal 3 Birr fee for Home Delivery will apply starting [Date]. Reiterate that Super Leader Pickup remains 100% free.
*   **T-1 Day (The Reminder):**
    *   *Message:* "Reminder: Delivery changes take effect tomorrow. Choose Free Pickup to save!"

## 3. High-Value Customer Objections & Resolutions

**Objection 1: The "Nickel-and-Dime" Reaction**
*   *Customer Thought:* "I spend thousands of Birr with ChipChip. Why are you charging me an extra 3 Birr?"
*   *Resolution:* Implement a **Minimum Basket Size Waiver**. Automatically waive the 3 Birr fee for any cart over a specific high-value threshold (e.g., 1000 Birr). This protects our highest-margin orders.

**Objection 2: Inconvenience / Entitlement**
*   *Customer Thought:* "I am too busy to walk to a Super Leader. I expect home delivery."
*   *Resolution:* Provide a **VIP Transition Grace Period**. Grant the top 5% of users a 30-day "Fee Free" pass to give them time to adjust, framing it as an exclusive loyalty reward.

**Objection 3: Quality & Trust at Pickup Locations**
*   *Customer Thought:* "Are my groceries safe sitting at someone else's house/shop?"
*   *Resolution:* Highlight Super Leader vetting and secure storage capabilities in the T-7 announcement. Include a "Satisfaction Guarantee" for the first 3 pickup orders.

## Summary Goal
Drive 40-50% of total volume to Super Leader Pickups to drastically reduce last-mile logistics costs, while retaining 95%+ of High-Value Customers through targeted waivers and grace periods.# ChipChip Delivery Fee Rollout - Option B Copy
