# Project: Self-Funding Workspace (Operation Token-Pay)

## Objective
Generate a consistent $100 - $200 per month in automated revenue to cover the OpenClaw workspace token and server costs.

## Constraints
1. **Zero Human Daily Effort**: Bruk will not dedicate daily hours to this. The AI team must handle generation, posting, and distribution.
2. **Infrastructure Limits**: Our AWS EC2 instance currently has ~4.8GB of free disk space and no dedicated GPU. Heavy 4K video rendering (like 3-hour podcast processing) will crash the server. We must focus on low-compute, high-leverage automation.
3. **Time to Revenue**: Needs a realistic path to hit the $100/mo threshold within 30-60 days.

## Research Vectors
The AI team is analyzing the following low-compute, fully-automated monetization engines:
1. **Automated AI Stock Photography**: Generating high-demand commercial images via API and auto-uploading them to Adobe Stock / Freepik via FTP.
2. **Faceless Theme Pages (Images/Carousels)**: Using the `ai-image-prompts` skill to auto-generate highly engaging "Mindset/Wealth" or "Deep Meaning" carousels for Instagram/Pinterest, monetized via Link-in-Bio high-ticket affiliates.
3. **Programmatic SEO / Niche Newsletter**: An automated Vercel-hosted blog that auto-publishes highly specific, SEO-optimized articles with software affiliate links (e.g., Notion, AI tools).
