## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

ChipChip proposes to partner with TechnoServe as the **primary implementation partner** for the Smart Souks program in Ethiopia — the localized version of Smart Duka 5.0 launched October 2025 with elea Foundation support.

**Our core value proposition:** ChipChip has **already built and is operating** the exact digital and supply chain infrastructure that Smart Duka 5.0 aims to deliver. We are not proposing to *build* Smart Souks — we already have them (SGL, souk profiles, merchant network). We are proposing to **scale and share** what's working.

This partnership delivers three simultaneous outcomes:
1. **For TechnoServe/elea:** Fastest path to 100+ souk impact, leveraging existing infrastructure
2. **For Souk Owners:** Immediate access to digital tools, training, supply chain optimization, and financial services
3. **For ChipChip:** Accelerated souk acquisition, program funding support for infrastructure scale, validated B2B merchant growth model

## 2. ABOUT TECHNOSERVE SMART DUKA 5.0

TechnoServe, in collaboration with the elea Foundation, launched Smart Duka 5.0 on October 30, 2025, in Nairobi, Kenya. The new phase brings the program to Ethiopia for the first time under the name **"Smart Souks."**

### Key Program Details (from official TechnoServe announcement):
- **Program Duration:** October 2025 – September 2028 (3 years)
- **First Ethiopia Cohort:** 100 Souks, recruited starting May 2026
- **Intervention Start:** June 2026
- **Funding:** elea Foundation for Ethics in Globalization
- **Kenya Track Record (Previous Phases):**
  - 50,000+ micro-retailers impacted
  - $5M+ capital mobilized
  - 10+ financial institution partnerships
  - 50+ manufacturer partnerships
  - 58 formalized business groups across 10 regional associations
  - $1M+ in aggregated goods over 3 years
  - 60% NDO operational self-sufficiency target

### Program Leadership:
- **Makeda T Tadesse** — TechnoServe Ethiopia Country Director
- **Kris Ansin** — TechnoServe Kenya Country Director

### Strategic Objectives:
1. Strengthen profitability and sustainability of micro-retailers
2. Equip souk owners with business and management skills
3. Build formalized business groups and regional associations
4. Aggregate purchasing for quantity discounts
5. Mobilize capital for micro-retail growth
6. Create resilient, innovative, inclusive retail sector
