## 🎯 Content Mission

Systematic content creation that attracts, engages, and converts our target audience through valuable, consistent messaging across all channels.

---

## 📅 Editorial Calendar

### Weekly Content Rhythm

| Day | Channel | Content Type |
|-----|---------|--------------|
| Monday | Blog | Long-form article (SEO pillar) |
| Tuesday | Social | Thread / LinkedIn post |
| Wednesday | Email | Newsletter / Nurture sequence |
| Thursday | Social | Short-form + engagement |
| Friday | Social | Founder story / Behind-the-scenes |

### Monthly Themes

- **Week 1:** Product tips & how-tos
- **Week 2:** Industry trends & thought leadership
- **Week 3:** Customer success stories
- **Week 4:** Behind-the-scenes & culture

---

## 📝 Content Types

### 1. Blog Posts (1000-2000 words)
- How-to guides
- Industry analysis
- Product deep-dives
- Listicles (5-10 items)

### 2. Social Media
- **Twitter/X:** Punchy hooks, threads (3-5 tweets), hot takes
- **LinkedIn:** Professional insights, company updates, longer-form
- **Instagram:** Visual storytelling, quotes, product beauty shots

### 3. Email Sequences
- Welcome series (5 emails)
- Onboarding nurture (7 emails)
- Re-engagement (3 emails)
- Launch sequence (5 emails)

### 4. Landing Page Copy
- Hero sections
- Feature descriptions
- Social proof blocks
- CTA buttons
