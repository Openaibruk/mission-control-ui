## 🎯 Campaign Type Templates

### 1. Product Launch Campaign
**Objective:** Drive awareness → consideration → adoption
- **TOFU:** Teaser campaign, influencer seeding, PR blast
- **MOFU:** Product demos, comparison guides, case study reveals
- **BOFU:** Limited-time offer, trial extension, referral incentive

### 2. Lead Generation Campaign
**Objective:** Capture qualified leads for pipeline
- **TOFU:** Educational content (guides, webinars), social proof
- **MOFU:** Email nurture sequence, retargeting, assessment tools
- **BOFU:** Consultation offer, demo request, ROI calculator

### 3. Brand Awareness Campaign
**Objective:** Increase visibility and mindshare
- **TOFU:** Thought leadership, community engagement, partnerships
- **MOFU:** Customer story features, industry report sponsorships
- **BOFU:** Event sponsorships, award submissions

### 4. Re-engagement Campaign
**Objective:** Win back inactive users
- **TOFU:** "We miss you" messaging, product updates
- **MOFU:** Special comeback offers, personalized recommendations
- **BOFU:** Exclusive loyalty rewards, urgency triggers

## 📋 Campaign Launch Checklist

- [ ] Define target audience & buyer persona
- [ ] Set campaign KPIs (CAC, LTV, Conversion Rate, CTR)
- [ ] Create messaging matrix (headline, body, CTA)
- [ ] Design creative assets (ads, landing pages, email)
- [ ] Set up tracking pixels & conversion events
- [ ] Build email sequences (if applicable)
- [ ] Schedule social posts & ad flights
- [ ] Prepare sales enablement materials
- [ ] Set up A/B tests for key variables
- [ ] Define reporting cadence

## 📊 Funnel Strategy Framework

| Stage | Goal | Key Tactics | Metrics |
|-------|------|-------------|---------|
| TOFU | Awareness | Content, PR, Social, Ads | Impressions, Reach, CPM |
| MOFU | Consideration | Nurture, Retargeting, Demos | MQLs, Engagement Rate |
| BOFU | Decision | Offers, Demos, Sales | SQLs, CAC, Close Rate |

## 📈 KPI Tracking Framework

- **Awareness:** impressions, reach, share of voice
- **Engagement:** CTR, time on site, pages per session
- **Conversion:** Lead-to-MQL ratio, conversion rate, CAC
- **Retention:** Churn rate, LTV, NPS
