# Driver Onboarding Standard Operating Procedure

**Company:** ChipChip  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-24  
**Owner:** Operations Team

---

## 1. Purpose

Standardize the driver onboarding process to ensure every new driver is properly vetted, trained, and equipped before their first delivery.

---

## 2. Onboarding Flow Overview

```
Application → Document Collection → Background Check → Training → Assessment → Approval → Active
```

---

## 3. Phase 1: Application & Document Collection

### Required Documents
| # | Document | Format | Notes |
|---|----------|--------|-------|
| 1 | National ID (Fayda or Kebele) | Photo/scan | Both sides |
| 2 | Driver's License | Photo/scan | Must be valid, Class 3+ |
| 3 | Vehicle Registration (if applicable) | Photo/scan | For own-vehicle drivers |
| 4 | Profile Photo | JPEG/PNG | Clear face, no sunglasses |
| 5 | Emergency Contact Form | Digital form | Name, phone, relationship |
| 6 | Bank Account / Telebirr Details | Text | For payment disbursement |

### Collection Process
1. Driver submits application via ChipChip app or in-person
2. Operations agent verifies document completeness within **24 hours**
3. Missing documents → automated follow-up SMS (max 3 reminders over 5 days)
4. Complete package → proceed to background check

---

## 4. Phase 2: Background & Vehicle Check

### Background Verification
- License validity check with Transport Authority
- Criminal record clearance (Addis Ababa Police Commission)
- Reference check: 1 previous employer or community reference

### Vehicle Inspection (own-vehicle drivers)
- Roadworthiness check: brakes, lights, tires, mirrors
- Insurance verification (valid third-party minimum)
- Cleanliness standards check
- Cargo capacity suitable for ChipChip orders

**SLA:** Background checks completed within **48 hours** of document submission.

---

## 5. Phase 3: Training Modules

### Module 1: Platform Training (30 min)
- App walkthrough: accepting orders, navigation, delivery confirmation
- Earnings structure and payment schedule
- How to report issues and contact support

### Module 2: Customer Service (20 min)
- Greeting and communication standards
- Handling delivery complaints
- Food safety basics (temperature, packaging integrity)

### Module 3: Safety & Compliance (20 min)
- Traffic rules and safe driving practices
- What to do in case of accident
- Emergency contact procedures
- COVID/hygiene protocols (if applicable)

### Module 4: Code of Conduct (10 min)
- Professional behavior expectations
- Zero tolerance: theft, harassment, intoxication on duty
- Consequences of violations

**Delivery:** In-person group session (weekly) OR video modules in-app

---

## 6. Phase 4: Assessment

- **Written quiz:** 15 questions, 80% pass mark
- **Practical test:** 1 supervised delivery run
- **Retake policy:** 1 free retake after 48-hour review period

---

## 7. Phase 5: Approval Workflow

| Step | Owner | SLA |
|------|-------|-----|
| Document completeness review | Operations Agent | 24 hrs |
| Background check clearance | Operations Lead | 48 hrs |
| Training completion confirmation | Training Coordinator | 48 hrs |
| Assessment pass verification | Training Coordinator | 24 hrs |
| Final approval & activation | Operations Manager | 24 hrs |

**Total target SLA:** Application to active driver in **≤ 7 business days**

---

## 8. Activation Checklist

- [ ] All documents verified and stored
- [ ] Background check cleared
- [ ] Training modules completed (4/4)
- [ ] Assessment passed (≥ 80%)
- [ ] Driver profile created in system
- [ ] Welcome kit issued (branded bag, ID badge)
- [ ] Payment details linked
- [ ] Added to driver WhatsApp/Telegram group
- [ ] First delivery zone assigned

---

## 9. Rejection Criteria

Application will be rejected if:
- Invalid or expired driver's license
- Failed background check (criminal offense related to driving/theft)
- Failed assessment twice
- Falsified documents
- Under 18 years of age

---

## 10. Escalation

| Issue | Escalate To | Response Time |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| Document dispute | Operations Lead | 24 hrs |
| Background check delay | Operations Manager | 12 hrs |
| Training accessibility | Training Coordinator | 24 hrs |
| System/app issues | Dev Team (Henok) | 4 hrs |

---

## Appendix: Welcome Kit Contents

1. Insulated delivery bag (branded)
2. ID badge with lanyard
3. Quick-reference card (support number, app tips)
4. Safety vest (for motorcycle drivers)
