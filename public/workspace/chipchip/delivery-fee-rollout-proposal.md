# 🏗 Task Architect Protocol: ChipChip Delivery Fee Rollout

## 1. Project Intent & Domain
*   **Domain:** Customer Communication & Pricing Strategy
*   **Intent:** Roll out a new 3 Birr home delivery fee while heavily incentivizing the *free* Super Leader pickup option.
*   **Research Check:** The messaging must be transparent but framed positively—highlighting the free community pickup as a "smart savings hack" (aligning perfectly with ChipChip's affordable, community-driven brand guidelines). We must carefully select which channels (Telegram, SMS, Push, In-App) to use to maximize reach without causing customer frustration.
