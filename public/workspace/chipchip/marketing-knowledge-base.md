## 🏢 Company Overview

**What is ChipChip?**  
Ethiopia's technology-first food supply chain platform. We connect farmers, suppliers, restaurants, and consumers through digitally orchestrated logistics — no warehouses, no middlemen.

**Mission:** "ChipChip stops touching products and starts controlling flows."

**Revenue Model:**
- Trade margin: 50%
- Financing/BNPL: 35%
- Supplier tools & ads: 15%

**Key Numbers (Feb 2026):**
- Monthly Sales: 7.4M ETB
- Monthly Orders: 83,731
- Monthly Volume: 153,989 kg
- Gross Margin: -16.68% (improving from -45% in January)
- Monthly Burn: 10.16M ETB

## 👥 Customer Segments

### Segment 1: Individual Consumers (B2C)
- **Volume:** 70.9% | **Revenue:** 72.4%
- **Order size:** ~2.14 kg / 88 ETB
- **Channel:** 85.7% via app
- **Pain:** High delivery fees (150 ETB), price volatility
- **Strategy:** Migrate to Super Leader pickup points

### Segment 2: Super Group Leaders (SGLs)
- **Volume:** 15.5% | **Revenue:** 11.6%
- **Order size:** 30+ kg
- **Role:** Community aggregation, local distribution
- **Incentive:** 5% GMV commission
- **Recruitment:** Driver-led, community mapping

### Segment 3: Restaurants & HORECA (B2B)
- **Volume:** 11% | **Revenue:** 15.8%
- **Order size:** 100+ kg
- **Pain:** Reliability, credit access, supply consistency
- **Strategy:** Contracts, BNPL, guaranteed SLA

### Segment 4: B2B Captains
- **Volume:** 2.6% | **Revenue:** 0.2%
- **Role:** Restaurant demand aggregation

## 🚀 Key Processes

### SGL Onboarding Flow
1. Recruitment (driver, social, referral)
2. Application (form or app)
3. Verification (phone + area check)
4. Welcome sequence (automated Day 0-7)
5. First group order (incentivized)
6. Ongoing support + commission tracking

### Restaurant Onboarding Flow
1. Outreach (walk-out sales, referral)
2. Menu + pricing setup
3. First order (20% discount)
4. Account manager assignment (90 days)
5. Weekly business review
6. Contract + BNPL terms

### Content Production Flow
1. Monday: Plan weekly content (calendar)
2. Tue-Thu: Create (copy, design, video)
3. Friday: Review + schedule
4. Weekend: Monitor engagement
5. Monday: Review metrics → adjust
