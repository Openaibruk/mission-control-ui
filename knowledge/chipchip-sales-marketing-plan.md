4. Execution Strategies
Operational Excellence

Fee Structure: Standard group members pay 10 ETB per order. Members ordering 5kg+ receive a free delivery coupon daily.
Aggregation Incentive: If total group volume exceeds 50kg, the entire drop is Free, eliminating per-order fees.
Pickup-First UI: Enforced system-wide to drive users toward Super Leader locations for centralized logistics.
Commission: 5% GMV Commission released to SL only after confirmed pickup—protecting the chain.
B2B2C: SUPER LEADER MODEL

MARGIN AUTOMATION RULE:
If CP1 fails, the order is automatically blocked by the system.
Product Mix Bundles: Combine High-Margin products with Low-Running products based on trends to push inventory while protecting CP1.
Volume Booster: Specialized offers designed to move stock fast and increase "Cost per KG" efficiency across all B2B routes.
Credit & Pricing: Credit % must be factored into pricing. No credit is extended without a Strong Legally Binding Contract.
