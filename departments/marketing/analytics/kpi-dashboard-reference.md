# KPI Dashboard Quick Reference

> At-a-glance guide to all marketing KPIs, targets, and where to find them.

---

## The Golden Metrics

> The three numbers that determine business health. Review DAILY.

| Metric | Target | Warning | Critical |
|--------|--------|---------|----------|
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | > 3:1 | 2.5-3:1 | < 2.5:1 |
| **CAC Payback Period** | < 12 months | 12-18 months | > 18 months |
| **ROAS** | > 3x | 2-3x | < 2x |

---

## Acquisition KPIs

| KPI | Target | Formula | Where to Find |
|-----|--------|---------|---------------|
| **CAC** | < $50 | Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers | CRM, Ads |
| **MQLs** | Per campaign target | Leads meeting BANT criteria | CRM |
| **MQL-to-SQL Rate** | > 20% | SQLs ÷ MQLs | CRM |
| **Lead-to-Customer** | 2-5% | Customers ÷ Total Leads | CRM |
| **Trial-to-Paid** | > 15% | Paid ÷ Trial Users | App, CRM |
| **CPL (Cost per Lead)** | < $50 | Spend ÷ Leads | Ads |

---

## Revenue & Growth KPIs

| KPI | Target | Formula | Where to Find |
|-----|--------|---------|---------------|
| **LTV** | > $500 | ARPU × Lifespan × Margin | CRM |
| **ARPU** | Growing MoM | Revenue ÷ Customers | CRM, Billing |
| **MRR** | Growing | Monthly Recurring Revenue | Billing |
| **Revenue per Campaign** | > 3x spend | Campaign Revenue ÷ Spend | Ads, CRM |
| **New Customers** | Per target | Count of new paying customers | CRM |

---

## Engagement KPIs

| KPI | Target | Formula | Where to Find |
|-----|--------|---------|---------------|
| **DAU/MAU** | > 20% | Daily Active ÷ Monthly Active | GA4, App |
| **Session Duration** | Growing | Avg time on site | GA4 |
| **Bounce Rate** | < 40% | Single-page ÷ Total | GA4 |
| **Pages per Session** | > 2.5 | Total pages ÷ Sessions | GA4 |
| **Email Open Rate** | 15-25% | Opens ÷ Delivered | Email Tool |
| **Email CTR** | 2-3% | Clicks ÷ Opens | Email Tool |
| **Ad CTR** | Varies | Clicks ÷ Impressions | Ads |

---

## Retention KPIs

| KPI | Target | Formula | Where to Find |
|-----|--------|---------|---------------|
| **Churn Rate** | < 5% | Lost customers ÷ Total | CRM |
| **NPS** | > 50 | Survey (0-10 scale) | Survey Tool |
| **Repeat Purchase Rate** | > 30% | 2+ orders ÷ Customers | CRM |
| **Net Revenue Retention** | > 100% | (MRR + Expansion - Churn) ÷ MRR | Billing |

## Quick Formulas

```
CAC = Total Marketing Spend / New Customers
LTV = ARPU × Customer Lifespan × Gross Margin
LTV:CAC = LTV / CAC
ROAS = Revenue / Ad Spend
CTR = Clicks / Impressions × 100
Conversion Rate = Conversions / Visitors × 100
```
