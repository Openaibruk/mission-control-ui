# Marketing Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

> Department workflows, checklists, and processes for consistent execution.

---

## 1. Campaign Launch SOP

### Pre-Launch Phase (T-7 days)

- [ ] **Creative assets approved**
  - All ads, images, videos reviewed
  - Brand compliance check passed
  - Legal review complete (if applicable)

- [ ] **Landing pages built and tested**
  - All links functional
  - Forms tested (submission flow)
  - Mobile responsive verified
  - Load time under 3 seconds

- [ ] **Email sequences scheduled**
  - All emails drafted and approved
  - Sequences timed correctly
  - Personalization tokens tested

- [ ] **Ad accounts funded**
  - Budget allocated and loaded
  - Campaigns built in all platforms
  - Pixel/tracking verified

- [ ] **Tracking configured**
  - UTM parameters defined
  - Conversion events mapped
  - Attribution model confirmed
  - Dashboards set up

- [ ] **Sales team briefed**
  - Campaign messaging aligned
  - Lead handling process confirmed
  - Follow-up playbooks distributed

### Launch Day (T-0)

- [ ] **Activate paid campaigns**
  - Turn on all scheduled ads
  - Verify delivery started
  - Monitor for delivery issues

- [ ] **Send launch email blast**
  - Verify send completed
  - Monitor deliverability
  - Watch for bounces/complaints

- [ ] **Post on social channels**
  - All platforms posted
  - Hashtags included
  - Engagement monitoring enabled

- [ ] **Monitor dashboards**
  - Set up real-time alerts
  - Watch for anomalies
  - Have troubleshooting plan ready

### Post-Launch (T+7 days)

- [ ] **Review initial performance**
  - Collect data from all platforms
  - Identify early trends
  - Document observations

- [ ] **Optimize underperformers**
  - Pause poor-performing ads
  - Increase budget on winners
  - A/B test new creatives

- [ ] **Document learnings**
  - Record what worked/didn't
  - Update future campaign briefs
  - Share with team

## 2. Content Publishing SOP

### Weekly Content Schedule

| Day | Channel | Content Type | Owner | Deadline |
|-----|---------|--------------|-------|----------|
| Monday | Blog | Educational post | Content team | Thursday |
| Tuesday | Email | Newsletter | Marketing | Friday |
| Wednesday | Social | Visual content | Social | Monday |
| Thursday | LinkedIn | Thought leadership | Content | Monday |
| Friday | Social | Light/engaging | Social | Thursday |

### Content Production Workflow

1. **Ideation** (Ongoing)
   - Review content calendar
   - Monitor trends and topics
   - Gather team input

2. **Briefing** (T-5 days)
   - Create content brief
   - Define target keyword
   - Outline key points
   - Assign writer

3. **Drafting** (T-3 days)
   - Write first draft
   - Include SEO elements
   - Add visual suggestions

4. **Review** (T-2 days)
   - Editorial review
   - Brand voice check
   - Fact-check

5. **Approval** (T-1 day)
   - Final approval
   - Asset collection
   - Format for publish

6. **Publish & Distribute** (T-0)
   - Publish to platform
   - Schedule social
   - Send email if applicable

### Content Quality Checklist

- [ ] Headline captures attention and includes keyword
- [ ] First paragraph hooks the reader
- [ ] Content delivers on headline promise
- [ ] 2-3 visuals included (or formatted for scannability)
- [ ] Clear CTA included
- [ ] SEO meta title and description set
- [ ] Internal links to 2+ related content
- [ ] Proofread for grammar and clarity

## 3. Weekly Marketing Standup

### Logistics
- **Frequency:** Every Monday
- **Duration:** 15 minutes
- **Attendees:** Marketing team, Sales rep (rotating)
- **Format:** Quick synchronous (Zoom/ Slack Huddle)

### Agenda

| Time | Item | Owner |
|------|------|-------|
| 0-5 min | Last week performance review | All |
| 5-10 min | This week campaign status | Campaign owners |
| 10-15 min | Blockers & needs | All |

### Pre-Standup Prep
- Update project tracker
- Note any blockers
- Bring 1 highlight from last week

## 6. Budget Management SOP

### Monthly Budget Process

1. **First week:** Review prior month spend
2. **Second week:** Adjust based on performance
3. **Third week:** Plan next month allocation
4. **Fourth week:** Finalize and document

### Budget Allocation Guidelines

| Category | Allocation | Review Frequency |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| Paid acquisition | 40% | Weekly |
| Content & SEO | 30% | Monthly |
| Email marketing | 20% | Monthly |
| Events & partnerships | 10% | Quarterly |

### Budget Approval Thresholds

| Spend Level | Approval Required |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Under $500 | Marketing lead |
| $500 - $5,000 | Marketing + Finance |
| Over $5,000 | Executive approval |

## 7. Crisis Communication SOP

### Social Media Crisis

1. **Assess** (Immediate)
   - Verify the issue
   - Determine severity (1-5 scale)
   - Identify affected parties

2. **Respond** (Within 1 hour)
   - Draft response (brand voice)
   - Get approval (based on severity)
   - Post response

3. **Monitor** (Ongoing)
   - Track sentiment
   - Respond to follow-ups
   - Document everything

4. **Post-Mortem** (Within 48 hours)
   - Document timeline
   - Identify prevention
   - Update procedures

### Escalation Contacts

| Issue Type | First Contact | Escalation |
|------------|---------------|------------|
| Product issue | Product marketing | VP Marketing |
| Legal matter | Legal | CEO |
| Media inquiry | PR lead | CEO |
| Security concern | Security | CTO |
