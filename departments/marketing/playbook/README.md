# Marketing Playbook Overview

> Quick reference guide for the marketing department.

---

## Purpose
This playbook provides standardized templates, procedures, and guidelines for marketing activities to ensure consistency and efficiency across the team.

---

## Contents

### 1. Audience Personas (`audience-personas.md`)
Detailed profiles of our three core customer segments:
- **Alex** — Tech-Savvy Explorer (25-40, digital-first, values efficiency)
- **Sam** — Value-Seeking Pragmatist (35-55, price-conscious, trust-driven)
- **Jordan** — Industry Professional (30-50, B2B, ROI-focused)

### 2. Campaign Templates (`campaign-templates.md`)
Reusable campaign frameworks:
- **Launch Campaign** — 4-6 week framework for new products
- **Content Campaign** — Quarterly rhythm for thought leadership
- **Growth Campaign** — Ongoing acquisition and scale strategy

### 3. Marketing SOP (`marketing-sop.md`)
Standard operating procedures:
- Campaign launch checklist
- Content publishing workflow
- Weekly standup format
- Monthly review process
- Lead management guidelines
- Budget management
- Crisis communication
