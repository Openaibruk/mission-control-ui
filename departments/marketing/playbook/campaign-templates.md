# Campaign Templates

> Reusable campaign frameworks for consistent execution.

## Template 1: Launch Campaign
**Purpose:** New product, feature, or major announcement launch
**Timeline:** 4-6 weeks
**Budget Scale:** Medium-High (concentrated spend)

### Campaign Phases

#### Week 1: Build Anticipation
| Activity | Channel | Deliverable |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Teaser social posts (3-5) | Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn | Visual teasers, countdown |
| "Sneak peek" email | Email list | Exclusive early access offer |
| Influencer seeding | Direct messages | Briefing document, early access |

#### Week 2: Generate Buzz
| Activity | Channel | Deliverable |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Influencer content drops | Influencer channels | Reviews, unboxing, tutorials |
| Press release distribution | PR wire, direct | Official release, quotes |
| Embargo media outreach | Trade publications | Review copies, interview offers |

#### Week 3: Launch Day
| Activity | Channel | Deliverable |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Launch email blast | Email | Full announcement, CTA |
| Social media blitz | All platforms | Launch post, Stories, threads |
| Press live | News sites | Coverage monitoring |
| Live demo/webinar | YouTube, Zoom | Launch event recording |

#### Week 4: Social Proof
| Activity | Channel | Deliverable |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Customer testimonials | Website, Ads | Video/text quotes |
| Case study release | Blog, LinkedIn | Detailed success story |
| User-generated content | Social | Repost customer posts |
| Retargeting ads | Meta, Google | Feature focus ads |

#### Weeks 5-6: Convert & Expand
| Activity | Channel | Deliverable |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Retargeting campaigns | Paid social, Display | Abandon cart, view through |
| Upgrade pushes | Email, In-app | Feature upgrade offers |
| Referral activation | Referral program | Incentive campaigns |
| Post-launch survey | Email | Feedback collection |

### Launch Campaign Assets Checklist

- [ ] Launch landing page
- [ ] Product demo video (60s, 3min versions)
- [ ] Press kit (logos, photos, quotes, fact sheet)
- [ ] Email sequence (teaser, launch, follow-up, thank you)
- [ ] Ad creative variants (5+ per channel)
- [ ] Social media posts (12+ pieces)
- [ ] Influencer briefing document
- [ ] FAQ document for sales team

### Launch KPIs

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Day 1 signups | Baseline + 200% | Registration tracking |
| Press coverage | 5+ outlets | Media monitoring |
| Social mentions | 100+ | Social listening |
| Email open rate | 25%+ | ESP tracking |
| Paid CAC | Under $50 (B2C) / $200 (B2B) | Ad platform |

## Template 2: Content Campaign
**Purpose:** Thought leadership, SEO, and demand generation
**Timeline:** Quarterly (rolling)
**Budget Scale:** Low-Medium (consistent, distributed)

### Campaign Rhythm

#### Monthly Framework

**Week 1: Research & Planning**
- Keyword research refresh
- Topic ideation session
- Content brief creation
- Asset gathering

**Week 2: Content Production**
- Blog posts (2-3)
- Social content batch (10-15 pieces)
- Email newsletter draft
- Video/podcast recording if applicable

**Week 3: Distribution**
- Publish blog content
- Schedule social posts
- Send newsletter
- Outreach for backlinks

**Week 4: Promotion & Optimization**
- Paid promotion of top performers
- Email follow-up to engaged users
- Content refresh for old posts
- Performance analysis

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | Purpose | Frequency | Examples |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|
| **Educational** | SEO, mid-funnel | 2-3x/week | How-tos, guides, tutorials |
| **Product** | Feature adoption | 1-2x/week | Use cases, tips, integrations |
| **Customer** | Social proof | 1-2x/week | Testimonials, case studies |
| **Industry** | Thought leadership | 1x/week | Trends, predictions, opinions |
| **Company** | Culture, updates | As needed | Milestones, team, news |

### Content Campaign Assets

- [ ] Content calendar (monthly)
- [ ] Content brief template
- [ ] Blog post template
- [ ] Social post templates
- [ ] Email newsletter template
- [ ] Distribution checklist

### Content KPIs

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Organic traffic growth | 15% QoQ | Google Analytics |
| Blog engagement | 3+ min avg time on page | GA4 |
| Email subscribers | 10% growth/quarter | ESP |
| Social engagement rate | 3%+ | Platform analytics |
| Backlinks gained | 5-10/month | Ahrefs/Moz |

## Template 3: Growth Campaign
**Purpose:** Scale acquisition and expand market reach
**Timeline:** Ongoing (quarterly cycles)
**Budget Scale:** High (sustained investment)

### Growth Channels

#### Paid Acquisition (40% of budget)
| Channel | Audience | Objective | Budget Split |
|---------|----------|-----------|--------------|
| Google Search | High intent | Conversions | 50% |
| Meta (FB/IG) | Broad, retarget | Lead gen | 30% |
| LinkedIn | B2B decision-makers | MQLs | 20% |

**Strategy:**
- Test 3-5 creative variants per channel
- 60% budget on proven winners
- 40% budget on testing new angles
- Weekly optimization cycles

#### Organic Growth (35% of budget)
| Activity | Focus | Output |
|----------|-------|--------|
| SEO | Long-tail keywords | 4+ blog posts/month |
| Email | Nurture sequences | 2+ sequences running |
| Social | Community building | Daily engagement |
| Referral | Word-of-mouth | Referral program active |

#### Partnerships (25% of budget)
| Program | Description | Expected Output |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| Co-marketing | Joint webinars, content | 2-3/quarter |
| Affiliate | Commission-based partners | 10+ active affiliates |
| Resellers | Channel partners | New market access |

### Growth Campaign Assets

- [ ] Paid media creative library
- [ ] Landing page variants
- [ ] A/B testing framework
- [ ] Attribution model setup
- [ ] Growth dashboard

### Growth KPIs

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| CAC | Decreasing QoQ | Finance reports |
| Monthly MRR growth | 10%+ | Billing system |
| Organic traffic ratio | 40%+ | Analytics |
| Paid conversion rate | 3%+ | Ad platforms |
| Referral contribution | 15%+ of new users | Referral tracking |

## Template Selection Guide

| Situation | Template | Adjustments |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| New product launch | Launch | 6-week timeline, high budget |
| Feature update | Content | 2-week focus, low budget |
| Market expansion | Growth | Quarterly cycle, high budget |
| Seasonal push | Growth + Content | Align with sales calendar |
| Brand awareness | Content | Focus on reach, not conversion |
| Competitor response | Launch (speed) | Compress to 2-3 weeks |
