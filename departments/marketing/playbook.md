# Marketing Playbook

> Comprehensive strategy guide for campaign execution, audience targeting, and channel optimization.

---

## 1. Audience Personas

### Persona A: "Alex" — The Tech-Savvy Explorer
- **Demographics:** 25-40, urban, digitally native, mid-to-high income
- **Behavior:** Researches extensively before purchase, values reviews and comparisons, mobile-first
- **Pain Points:** Overwhelmed by options, fears making wrong choice, wants validation
- **Motivation:** Efficiency, staying ahead of trends, smart spending
- **Channels:** Instagram, YouTube, email, podcasts
- **Messaging:** Data-driven, social proof, "new & improved"

### Persona B: "Sam" — The Value-Seeking Pragmatist
- **Demographics:** 35-55, suburban, family-oriented, budget-conscious
- **Behavior:** Price-sensitive, responds to deals, word-of-mouth driven
- **Pain Points:** Budget constraints, skepticism of marketing claims, time scarcity
- **Motivation:** Reliability, value for money, trusted brands
- **Channels:** Facebook, email newsletters, local events
- **Messaging:** Savings, reliability, "trusted by thousands"

### Persona C: "Jordan" — The Industry Professional
- **Demographics:** 30-50, B2B context, decision-maker, enterprise-focused
- **Behavior:** Solution-oriented, ROI-focused, lengthy evaluation process
- **Pain Points:** Integration challenges, vendor risk, proving business case
- **Motivation:** Career advancement, operational efficiency, competitive edge
- **Channels:** LinkedIn, industry publications, webinars, email
- **Messaging:** Case studies, ROI calculators, enterprise-grade guarantees

## 2. Funnel Framework

### Funnel Stages

**AWARENESS**
- Brand messaging & positioning
- Top-of-funnel content (blogs, social, ads)
- Goal: Capture attention, introduce brand
- Key Metrics: Impressions, reach, brand recall surveys

**CONSIDERATION**
- Educational content (guides, webinars, comparisons)
- Email nurturing sequences
- Goal: Build trust, demonstrate value
- Key Metrics: Email open rates, content engagement, time on site

**DECISION**
- Case studies, testimonials, free trials
- Sales enablement materials
- Goal: Convert interest to action
- Key Metrics: Conversion rate, trial-to-paid, cost per lead

**RETENTION**
- Onboarding, engagement campaigns, upsell
- Community, loyalty programs
- Goal: Drive loyalty, maximize LTV
- Key Metrics: NPS, repeat purchase rate, churn rate, LTV

## 3. Campaign Templates

### 3.1 Launch Campaign (New Product/Feature)

**Timeline:** 4-6 weeks

| Week | Activity | Channel | Goal |
|------|----------|---------|------|
| 1 | Teaser campaign, "sneak peek" content | Social, email | Build anticipation |
| 2 | Influencer seeding, embargo reviews | Influencers, PR | Generate buzz |
| 3 | Launch day: press release, live demo | All channels | Maximum visibility |
| 4 | User testimonials, case study release | Website, ads | Social proof |
| 5-6 | Retargeting, upgrade pushes | Paid, email | Convert & expand |

**Key Assets Needed:**
- Launch landing page
- Product demo video
- Press kit
- Email sequences (teaser, launch, follow-up)
- Ad creative variants

---

### 3.2 Growth Campaign (Scale & Acquire)

**Timeline:** Ongoing (quarterly cycles)

**Focus Areas:**

1. **Paid Acquisition**
   - Platform: Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn (B2B)
   - Strategy: Test 3-5 creative variants per channel
   - Budget allocation: 60% proven, 40% testing

2. **Content Engine**
   - 2-3 blog posts/week targeting mid-funnel keywords
   - Monthly webinar or live Q&A
   - Weekly newsletter

3. **Referral & Partnerships**
   - Partner co-marketing (webinars, bundle offers)
   - Referral program with incentive structure
   - Affiliate network activation

**Growth KPIs:**
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- Monthly new users/monthly recurring revenue growth
- Organic traffic ratio

---

### 3.3 Retention Campaign (Keep & Expand)

**Timeline:** Continuous, with quarterly campaigns

**Core Programs:**

| Program | Frequency | Channel | Target |
|---------|-----------|---------|--------|
| Welcome series | Day 0-14 | Email | New customers |
| Re-engagement win-back | Monthly | Email, push | Dormant users |
| Loyalty rewards | Ongoing | All | High-value customers |
| Upsell/cross-sell | Quarterly | Email, in-app | Engaged base |
| Feedback loop | Bi-annual | Survey | All customers |

**Retention KPIs:**
- Churn rate (monthly/annual)
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- NPS score

## 4. Channel Strategy

### Channel Matrix

| Channel | Best For | Content Type | Typical ROI |
|---------|----------|--------------|-------------|
| **Email** | Nurture, retention, conversion | Newsletters, sequences, promotions | 36:1 |
| **Social (Meta)** | Awareness, consideration | Visual content, stories, reels | 4-6:1 |
| **Google Ads** | Intent capture, consideration | Search, display, shopping | 4:1 |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B, thought leadership | Articles, case studies, ads | 3-5:1 |
| **Content/SEO** | Long-term growth, authority | Blog, guides, resource pages | 8-12:1 (long-term) |
| **Webinars** | Consideration, B2B | Live events, on-demand | 5:1 |

### Channel Selection by Persona

| Persona | Primary | Secondary | Tertiary |
|---------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Alex (Explorer) | Instagram, YouTube | Email, Podcasts | Google Search |
| Sam (Pragmatist) | Facebook, Email | Local events | Google Display |
| Jordan (Professional) | LinkedIn | Industry publications | Webinars, Email |

## 5. Marketing SOP

### 5.1 Campaign Launch Checklist

**Pre-Launch (T-7 days)**
- [ ] Creative assets approved
- [ ] Landing pages built and tested
- [ ] Email sequences scheduled
- [ ] Ad accounts funded and campaigns built
- [ ] Tracking/UTMs configured
- [ ] Sales team briefed on campaign

**Launch Day (T-0)**
- [ ] Activate paid campaigns
- [ ] Send launch email blast
- [ ] Post on social channels
- [ ] Monitor dashboards for anomalies

**Post-Launch (T+7 days)**
- [ ] Review initial performance data
- [ ] Optimize underperforming ads
- [ ] Document learnings

### 5.2 Content Publishing Schedule

| Day | Channel | Content Type |
|-----|---------|--------------|
| Monday | Blog | Educational post |
| Tuesday | Email | Newsletter |
| Wednesday | Social | Visual content |
| Thursday | LinkedIn | Thought leadership article |
| Friday | Social | Light/engaging content |

### 5.3 Weekly Marketing Standup

**Attendees:** Marketing team, Sales rep
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Agenda:**
1. Last week performance review (5 min)
2. This week campaign status (5 min)
3. Blockers & needs (5 min)

### 5.4 Monthly Review Process

1. **Data Collection** (1st week): Pull all platform reports
2. **Analysis** (2nd week): Identify trends, wins, and areas for improvement
3. **Reporting** (3rd week): Present to stakeholders
4. **Planning** (4th week): Adjust next month's plan based on learnings
