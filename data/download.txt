Delivery Fee & Pick-Up Hub Promotion Guideline
1. Main Goal of the Promotion
The promotion should achieve three things:
1. Inform customers that delivery fee will start from ---- birr.
2. Introduce the Pick-Up Hub option as an alternative.
3. Encourage some customers to become Pick-Up Leaders in their area.
The message should focus on choice and benefits, not only the fee.
________________


2. Key Message to Communicate
Customers will now have two options:
Option 1 – Home Delivery
* Order delivered to your home
* Delivery fee will apply

Option 2 – Pick-Up Hub
   * Collect order from a nearby hub
   * Free delivery cost & 0 packaging fee
   * Promote To prepare their own packaging since we will not charge them 

Message tone:
“You now have more delivery options.”
Not:
“Delivery fee is starting.”
3. Benefits of Pick-Up Option (Must be Highlighted)
Customers must clearly understand why pick-up is good.
Advantages
1. Save Money
      * Free delivery cost & 0 packaging fee
2. Faster Collection
      * Orders arrive at the hub earlier.

3. Reliable Pickup
         * No waiting for driver timing.

4. Flexible Time
            * Pick up when it’s convenient.

5. Community Buying
               * Order together with neighbors and save more.

6. Opportunity to Earn
                  * Customers can become Pick-Up Leaders and earn commission.

________________





4. Content Types Needed
A. Educational Posts
Explain the new system simply.
Examples:
                     * “Two Ways to Receive Your Order”
                     * “Home Delivery vs Pick-Up Hub”

Goal: Awareness.
________________


B. Tutorial Content
Show how to choose pick-up or delivery in the app.
Tutorial should include:
                        * Where to select delivery option
                        * How to find nearest pick-up hub
                        * How to collect orders

________________


C. Pick-Up Leader Opportunity
Promote opportunity for customers.
Message example:
“Do you want to earn extra income?
Become a Pick-Up Hub Leader in your area.”





Explain benefits:
                           * Commission from orders
                           * More customers in their area
                           * Community service

6. Promotion Channels
Use multiple channels because customers behave differently.
Channels:
                                                                     * Social Media (Facebook, Telegram)
                                                                     * In-app messages
                                                                     * SMS
                                                                     * Group leader communication
                                                                     * Customer support explanation











7. Communication Tone
Communication should be:
Simple
Clear
Friendly
Educational
Avoid complicated explanations.
Use messages like:
“You now have more delivery choices.”
“Pick up nearby and save delivery cost.”
“Become a pick-up leader and earn.”
________________
