# ChipChip Driver Onboarding Checklist

**Version:** 1.0  
**Created:** 2026-03-16  
**Owner:** ChipChip Logistics Team

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Registration (Day 0)

### Document Collection
- [ ] Valid driver's license (front and back scan)
- [ ] Vehicle registration document
- [ ] Vehicle insurance certificate (current)
- [ ] National ID or passport copy
- [ ] Profile photo (clear, professional)
- [ ] Emergency contact information
- [ ] Bank account details for payments

### Eligibility Verification
- [ ] License validity: minimum 2 years driving experience
- [ ] Age requirement: 21+ years old
- [ ] Vehicle age: less than 10 years old
- [ ] Clean driving record (no major violations in past 3 years)
- [ ] Background check passed

---

## Phase 2: Account Setup (Day 1)

### Digital Onboarding
- [ ] Download ChipChip Driver app
- [ ] Create driver account with verified phone number
- [ ] Upload all documents to app
- [ ] Set up payment/payout method
- [ ] Review and accept Terms of Service
- [ ] Review and accept Privacy Policy

### Compliance
- [ ] Complete mandatory safety training module
- [ ] Pass basic knowledge test (traffic rules, app usage)
- [ ] Sign Driver Agreement contract
- [ ] Acknowledge Code of Conduct

---

## Phase 3: Training & Orientation (Day 1-2)

### App Training
- [ ] Navigate the driver dashboard
- [ ] Accept and manage delivery requests
- [ ] Use GPS navigation within app
- [ ] Handle customer communication (chat/call)
- [ ] Process delivery confirmation (photo upload)
- [ ] Report issues and escalate problems

### Operational Training
- [ ] Package handling best practices
- [ ] Temperature-sensitive delivery procedures
- [ ] Cash handling and COD procedures
- [ ] Customer service standards
- [ ] Safety protocols and emergency procedures

### Assessment
- [ ] Complete training quiz (minimum 80% score)
- [ ] Shadow delivery with experienced driver (optional)
- [ ] First supervised delivery

---

## Phase 4: First Week Support (Day 3-7)

### Daily Check-ins
- [ ] Day 3: First independent delivery — team lead check-in
- [ ] Day 5: Performance review (delivery time, ratings)
- [ ] Day 7: Week 1 review meeting

### Performance Metrics
- [ ] Minimum 5 deliveries completed
- [ ] Average rating ≥ 4.0 stars
- [ ] Delivery success rate ≥ 95%
- [ ] Response time < 2 minutes for new requests

---

## Phase 5: Full Activation (Day 8+)

- [ ] Remove new driver flag from profile
- [ ] Enable full delivery zone access
- [ ] Add to driver community group
- [ ] Schedule monthly performance review
- [ ] Welcome to the team notification
