## Welcome to ChipChip! 🚗

You're now part of the ChipChip logistics team. This training manual covers everything you need to know to deliver excellent service and grow with us.

---

## Module 1: The ChipChip Platform

### What is ChipChip?
ChipChip is a technology platform connecting drivers with delivery requests. We serve customers across the city with fast, reliable deliveries.

### How It Works
1. Customer places an order through ChipChip
2. System assigns delivery to nearest available driver
3. Driver accepts, picks up, and delivers
4. Customer confirms delivery and rates service

### Your Role
- Accept and complete delivery requests efficiently
- Handle packages with care
- Provide friendly, professional customer service
- Maintain your vehicle and app in good working condition

---

## Module 2: Using the Driver App

### Getting Started
1. Open the app and go online (toggle "Available")
2. Wait for delivery requests (you'll hear a notification sound)
3. Review request details: pickup location, drop-off, estimated earnings
4. Accept or decline within 30 seconds

### During a Delivery
1. Navigate to pickup location (use in-app GPS or your preferred maps app)
2. Confirm pickup — take photo of package
3. Navigate to drop-off location
4. Confirm delivery — take photo of delivered package
5. Rate the delivery experience

### Key Features
- **Earnings Dashboard**: Track daily/weekly earnings
- **Delivery History**: Review past deliveries
- **Support Chat**: Reach our team instantly
- **Issue Reporting**: Flag problems in real-time

## Module 3: Delivery Standards

### Time Targets
| Delivery Type | Target Time |
|--------------|-------------|
| Standard (within city) | 45 minutes |
| Express | 20 minutes |
| Scheduled | Within 15-min window |

### Package Handling
- Always verify package matches order details
- Handle fragile items with extra care
- Keep packages clean and dry
- Never leave packages unattended
- If package is damaged, report immediately before delivery

### Customer Interaction
- Greet customer by name
- Confirm delivery address before handing over
- Be polite and professional
- If customer not available, follow the no-contact protocol
- Never request additional payment from customers

---

## Module 4: Safety & Compliance

### Driving Safety
- Always follow traffic rules — no exceptions
- Use seatbelt at all times
- No phone usage while driving (use hands-free)
- Take breaks every 3 hours of continuous driving
- Do not drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs

### Personal Safety
- Trust your instincts — if a location feels unsafe, contact support
- Share your live location during deliveries (optional)
- Keep your phone charged (minimum 30%)
- Carry a vehicle emergency kit

### Data & Privacy
- Never share customer information with third parties
- Do not photograph customers without consent
- Delete delivery address info after completion
- Report any data breaches immediately

## Module 5: Earnings & Payments

### How You Earn
- **Base fare**: Per delivery completed
- **Distance bonus**: For deliveries over 5km
- **Peak bonus**: Higher rates during peak hours
- **Rating bonus**: Monthly bonus for 4.5+ average rating

### Payment Schedule
- Weekly payouts every Monday
- Minimum payout: 500 ETB
- Instant cashout available (once per day)

### Deductions
- Platform fee: 15% per delivery
- No hidden charges

## Module 6: Problem Resolution

### Common Issues

**Package damaged during transit:**
1. Take photo of damage
2. Contact support immediately
3. Do not attempt delivery
4. Return to pickup location if instructed

**Customer not available:**
1. Wait 5 minutes
2. Try calling customer (3 attempts, 2-min intervals)
3. Contact support for next steps
4. Do not leave package unattended

**Vehicle breakdown:**
1. Contact support immediately
2. If carrying a package, secure it safely
3. Arrange alternative delivery if possible
4. Document the incident

**Wrong address:**
1. Contact customer through app
2. If unresolvable, contact support
3. Do not deliver to unverified addresses
