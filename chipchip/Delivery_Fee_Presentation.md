## Slide 2: Executive Summary
**Goal:** Introduce home delivery fee while maximizing free pickup adoption
- **Change:** Home delivery now costs 3 Birr per order
- **Alternative:** Customers can select "Free Pickup" from their local Super Leader
- **Positioning:** "Smart savings hack" for the community
- **Impact:** Protect margins without alienating budget-conscious customers

**We are NOT adding a fee—we are adding a feature!**
- Framing: "We're excited to bring you FREE Super Leader Pickup as a community-powered savings option."
- The 3 Birr is for *optional convenience* (home delivery)
- Keeps ChipChip affordable and community-focused
- No change to existing service quality

## Slide 6: High-Value Customer Objections & Resolutions
### Objection: "Nickel-and-diming after loyal shopping"
**Response:** 
- Acknowledge loyalty; offer a 30-day grace period (fee-free) for top 5% of customers
- Personalized outreach: "We value you—here's 30 days of free home delivery"

### Objection: "Inconvenience; I want my stuff delivered"
**Response:**
- Promote a "Minimum Basket Size Waiver": Free delivery on orders > X Birr
- Bonuses for choosing pickup (e.g., double loyalty points)
