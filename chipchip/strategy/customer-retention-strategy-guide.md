## 1. The Retention Framework

### The ChipChip Retention Flywheel

```
Great Experience → Trust → Repeat Orders → Group Leadership →
Community Growth → Better Prices → Great Experience (loop)
```

Every retention initiative should strengthen at least one link in this chain.

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target | Current | Status |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------|--------|
| Day-7 Retention | % of new customers who order again within 7 days | > 40% | ___ | |
| Day-30 Retention | % of customers with 2+ orders in first 30 days | > 25% | ___ | |
| Monthly Active Rate | % of registered customers who ordered in past 30 days | > 35% | ___ | |
| Churn Rate | % of previously active customers with no order in 60 days | < 20% | ___ | |

## 2. New Customer Onboarding (Days 0-30)

### Day 0: First Order

**Goal:** Make the first experience exceptional.

- Welcome email with first-order discount code (if not already used)
- Push notification: "Your ChipChip journey begins! Track your order in real-time"
- In-app onboarding tour (3 screens max — how group buying works)
- Include a small freebie or handwritten note in first delivery (when feasible)

### Day 1: Post-Delivery Follow-Up

- Automated satisfaction survey (keep to 3 questions max)
- If rating ≥ 4: "Share your experience" prompt → review/UGC
- If rating ≤ 3: Auto-escalate to customer service for personal outreach

### Day 3: Education & Engagement

- Email: "3 things you didn't know about ChipChip" (group buying tips, how to start your own group, referral program)
- Push notification: "Your next group buy is waiting" — show items similar to first order

### Day 7: Re-Engagement Nudge

- If no second order: Personalized email with items they browsed + a "come back" incentive ($5 credit)
- If second order placed: Thank you message + invite to join/create a community group

### Day 14: Community Integration

- Invite to neighborhood group (WhatsApp/Telegram)
- Highlight active group buys in their area
- Share referral program details with personalized referral link

### Day 30: Milestone Check

- "Your first month with ChipChip" summary (orders placed, money saved, etc.)
- If 3+ orders: Unlock loyalty tier benefits
- If 1 order: Win-back campaign activation
- If 0 orders since Day 1: Serious re-engagement push

## 3. Ongoing Engagement Strategies

### 3.1 Loyalty Program: "ChipChip Rewards"

| Tier | Requirement | Benefits |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Fresh | 0-2 orders | Standard pricing, basic support |
| Regular | 3-9 orders | 2% cashback on orders, early access to new products |
| VIP | 10-24 orders | 5% cashback, free delivery, priority support, monthly surprise gift |
| Champion | 25+ orders | 8% cashback, free delivery, dedicated account manager, quarterly gift box, product input sessions |

### 3.2 Personalization Engine

**Product Recommendations:**
- Based on past orders, browsing history, and seasonal patterns
- "You might love these" section in app and email
- "Frequently bought together" suggestions

**Timing Optimization:**
- Send notifications when the customer is most likely to order (based on historical pattern)
- Remind before their typical order day: "Time for your weekly [category] restock?"

**Content Personalization:**
- Recipe suggestions based on purchased ingredients
- Storage tips for products they've ordered
- New arrivals in categories they frequent

### 3.3 Win-Back Campaigns

**Trigger:** Customer hasn't ordered in 30+ days

| Stage | Timing | Channel | Message |
|-------|--------|---------|---------|
| Soft nudge | Day 30 | Push notification | "Miss you! Here's what's new this week" |
| Incentive offer | Day 45 | Email + SMS | "Come back for 15% off your next order" |
| Urgency push | Day 60 | Email | "Your $10 credit expires in 3 days" |
| Final attempt | Day 90 | SMS + Email | "We'd love to have you back. 20% off, no strings attached" |
| Archive | Day 120 | — | Move to dormant segment, reduce communication frequency |

### 3.4 Referral Program

**Structure:**
- Referrer gets: $10 credit when referee places first order
- Referee gets: $5 off first order
- Bonus: If referee becomes a regular (3+ orders), referrer gets additional $15 credit

**Promotion:**
- In-app referral widget (persistent but non-intrusive)
- Post-order thank you screen with referral share option
- Monthly "top referrer" leaderboard with prizes

### 3.5 Group Leader Empowerment

Group leaders are the highest-value customers. Treat them accordingly:

- **Dedicated support channel** — priority response within 30 minutes
- **Enhanced dashboard** — group analytics, member management tools
- **Exclusive pricing** — additional 3-5% discount on group orders
- **Early access** — new products, features, promotions
- **Recognition** — "Top Group Leader" badges, featured spotlights
- **Feedback loop** — quarterly calls with Product team to shape roadmap

## 4. Churn Prevention Signals & Interventions

| Signal | Severity | Automated Response | Manual Follow-Up |
|--------|----------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Decreased order frequency | Medium | Personalized "what's new" email | If no response in 7 days → phone call |
| Browsed but didn't order (3x) | Medium | Cart reminder + small incentive | — |
| Submitted complaint | High | Immediate CS ticket + apology | Resolution + goodwill credit within 24h |
| Canceled order | Medium | "Something wrong?" survey | Review response → address root cause |
| Left negative review | High | Auto-assign to senior CS agent | Personal call within 4 hours |
| Removed payment method | High | "We'll miss you" email + save incentive | — |
| Deleted account | Critical | Exit survey (why?) | — |

## 7. Quarterly Review Process

1. Pull all retention metrics and compare to targets.
2. Analyze cohort retention curves — which acquisition channels produce the stickiest customers?
3. Survey churned customers (sample of 50+) — qualitative churn reasons.
4. A/B test results review — which interventions worked best?
5. Update strategy based on findings.
6. Present retention report to leadership with recommendations.
