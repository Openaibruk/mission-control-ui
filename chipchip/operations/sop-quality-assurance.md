# Standard Operating Procedure: Quality Assurance & Product Standards

**Document ID:** CHIP-OPS-004
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-03-16
**Owner:** Quality Assurance Team
**Review Cycle:** Quarterly

---

## 1. Purpose

To establish and maintain consistent quality standards for all products sold through ChipChip, ensuring customer satisfaction, food safety, and supplier accountability.

## 2. Scope

All products listed on the ChipChip platform, including fresh produce, packaged goods, dairy, meat, and specialty items.

## 3. Quality Standards by Category

### 3.1 Fresh Produce

| Criterion | Standard | Measurement |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Appearance | No visible mold, bruising >10% of surface, or wilting | Visual inspection |
| Ripeness | Appropriate for delivery window + 2 days consumption | Color, firmness, aroma |
| Size | Within stated size category; consistent across batch | Weight/diameter check |
| Cleanliness | Free from excessive soil, debris, or foreign matter | Visual inspection |
| Shelf Life | Minimum 3 days from delivery date | Expiration/stalk freshness |

### 3.2 Dairy & Eggs

| Criterion | Standard | Measurement |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Temperature | Maintained at 1-4°C throughout supply chain | Cold chain log verification |
| Expiration | Minimum 5 days from delivery date | Date label check |
| Packaging | Sealed, no cracks or leaks | Visual inspection |
| Appearance | No discoloration, separation, or off-odors | Sensory evaluation |

### 3.3 Meat & Seafood

| Criterion | Standard | Measurement |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Temperature | Frozen: -18°C or below; Fresh: 0-4°C | Temperature log |
| Expiration | Minimum 3 days from delivery (fresh); 30+ days (frozen) | Date label check |
| Color | Characteristic of species; no browning or graying | Visual inspection |
| Odor | Fresh, mild, characteristic smell | Sensory evaluation |
| Packaging | Vacuum-sealed or securely wrapped, no leaks | Visual inspection |

### 3.4 Packaged / Shelf-Stable Goods

| Criterion | Standard | Measurement |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Expiration | Minimum 30 days from delivery date | Date label check |
| Packaging | Intact seals, no dents, no swelling | Visual inspection |
| Labeling | Complete ingredient list, allergen info, nutrition facts, origin | Label review |
| Weight/Volume | Within 2% of stated net weight | Scale measurement |

## 4. Quality Checkpoints

### Checkpoint 1: Supplier Pre-Shipment
- **Who:** Supplier's quality team
- **When:** Before items are loaded for pickup
- **Method:** Supplier self-assessment using ChipChip QA form
- **Documentation:** Submitted via supplier dashboard

### Checkpoint 2: Hub Intake Inspection
- **Who:** ChipChip QA Inspector
- **When:** Upon arrival at distribution hub
- **Method:** Random sampling — inspect 10% of items per SKU, minimum 5 units
- **Pass criteria:** ≥95% of sampled items meet standards
- **Fail action:** Reject entire batch for that SKU; supplier notified immediately

### Checkpoint 3: Pre-Delivery Spot Check
- **Who:** Hub Operations Coordinator
- **When:** During order packing
- **Method:** Visual check of each order for obvious issues
- **Pass criteria:** No visible defects, correct items, proper packaging
- **Fail action:** Replace item from inventory or flag for customer communication

### Checkpoint 4: Customer Receipt
- **Who:** Customer
- **When:** At delivery
- **Method:** Customer inspection upon receipt
- **Complaint window:** 24 hours with photo evidence

## 5. Supplier Quality Scoring

Each supplier receives a monthly quality score (0-100) based on:

| Factor | Weight | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Product quality compliance | 40% | QA checkpoint pass rate |
| Customer complaints | 25% | Complaints per 100 orders |
| Fulfillment accuracy | 20% | Correct items/quantities delivered |
| Documentation compliance | 10% | Timely submission of required docs |
| Responsiveness | 5% | Response time to quality issues |

### Score Tiers & Actions

| Score | Tier | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| 90-100 | Platinum | Featured placement, priority orders, extended payment terms |
| 75-89 | Gold | Standard status, regular monitoring |
| 60-74 | Silver | Quality improvement plan required, increased inspection rate |
| Below 60 | At Risk | Formal warning, mandatory remediation within 30 days |
| Below 50 (or repeat offense) | Suspended | Account suspended pending review |

## 6. Non-Conformance Handling

### Minor Issue (isolated, low impact)
1. Document in supplier incident log.
2. Notify supplier with specific feedback.
3. No immediate action; monitored for patterns.

### Moderate Issue (recurring or moderate impact)
1. Formal non-conformance report (NCR) issued.
2. Supplier must respond with root cause analysis within 48 hours.
3. Corrective action plan required within 7 days.
4. Increased inspection rate (25% sampling) for next 30 days.

### Major Issue (safety concern, widespread defect, or fraud)
1. Immediate batch hold — all items quarantined.
2. Supplier account suspended pending investigation.
3. Customer notification if any distributed items affected.
4. Regulatory reporting if food safety violation.
5. Supplier must submit full corrective action plan before reinstatement.

## 7. Documentation & Records

- QA inspection reports (retained 2 years)
- Supplier quality scorecards (retained 2 years)
- Non-conformance reports (retained 3 years)
- Customer complaint records related to quality (retained 2 years)
- Cold chain temperature logs (retained 1 year)
