# ChipChip Sourcing Strategy: 3-Month Execution Plan (RESET 2026 Aligned)

**Author:** Nahom (VP Strategy / Marketing) & Nova (Chief of Staff)
**Date:** March 19, 2026
**Framework:** CHIPCHIP RESET 2026 (Asset-Light, 0% B2C, Flow Control)

---

## Executive Context

The previous sourcing model was built for a legacy, warehouse-heavy B2C operation that accepted double-digit arrival/warehouse damage losses. 
Under **CHIPCHIP RESET 2026**, the Sourcing Team's role fundamentally changes: **Stop touching products and start controlling flows.**

We are transitioning from a *speculative buying team* to an **asset-light flow designer and SLA enforcer.** Sourcing will no longer negotiate individual bags of carrots; it will match aggregated Tier 2 (Super Leader, 30kg) and Tier 3 (B2B Captain, 100kg+) demand directly to Supplier Micro-DCs.

---

## Month 1: Kill the Warehouse & Shift to Demand-Backed Flow

**Objective:** Eliminate the 10% combined arrival/warehouse damage leak by transitioning to an asset-light, Just-In-Time (JIT) model.

1. **Zero-Speculation Sourcing:**
   - Halt all speculative purchasing based on historical run rates.
   - Sourcing is now strictly triggered by **Aggregated Weekly Demand Forecasts** generated by B2B Captains and Super Leaders.
2. **Push QC Upstream (Micro-DC Shift):**
   - Shift the grading/QC protocol (e.g., Phase 1 Red Onion specs) out of the ChipChip central warehouse and into the **Supplier-owned Micro-DC** or farm gate.
   - The Sourcing Team's new KPI is **Supplier SLA Compliance** (Zero Warehouse Rejections because the warehouse no longer holds stock).
3. **Phase-Out Legacy Flash Sales:**
   - Since we no longer hold inventory past 48 hours, the SPM (Strategic Product Manager) transitions from "Flash Sale Manager" to "Flow Controller," re-routing misaligned supply directly to secondary wholesale partners before it enters our logistics chain.

---

## Month 2: Establish the Trust & Data Infrastructure

**Objective:** Turn the Sourcing Team into an open data layer that ranks, matches, and disciplines suppliers.

1. **Supplier Reputation Network (SRN):**
   - Build a real-time, transparent dashboard ranking farmers and cooperatives based on:
     - *Defect Rates* (Accuracy against Grade A/B/C specs).
     - *Fulfillment Reliability* (On-time dispatch matching SGL demand drops).
   - Top-tier suppliers gain preferred visibility when B2B Captains query the Supply Marketplace.
2. **Dynamic Predictive Bidding:**
   - Replace the legacy 48-hour pre-dispatch notification with a **Forward-Contracting Model**. 
   - Using AI-powered demand forecasting (Platform Tools SaaS), the Sourcing Team locks in "Floor & Ceiling" prices with Tier-1 SRN suppliers for 3-month windows, bypassing volatile Sunday wholesale markets.
3. **Discipline Pricing (The Golden Rule):**
   - Sourcing aligns with Marketing: We do not negotiate demand. We price it into discipline. 
   - Suppliers who can pre-box SKUs for direct Super Leader hub drops (bypassing consolidation) receive a +5% margin share.

---

## Month 3: BNPL Financing & B2B Integration

**Objective:** Monetize the supply chain through financing and solidify the "Veins of the Economy" end-state (100% B2B/B2B2C).

1. **Supplier BNPL Integration:**
   - Roll out short-cycle (7-14 days max) credit products for top-tier SRN suppliers to help them scale production or improve Micro-DC boxing capabilities.
   - **Hard Rule:** Auto-block on defaults. No emotional overrides. This generates a new ~35% revenue stream outside of the standard Trade Margin.
2. **B2B Captain Direct Routing:**
   - Finalize the **Direct DC → Restaurants (B2B)** flow. 
   - Sourcing fully steps out of the middle. The Sourcing Team's software (Trade Margin Spread) automatically matches a restaurant's 100kg+ order directly with a farm's Micro-DC.
   - ChipChip takes its ~2 ETB/kg spread strictly as a Settlement Engine and Flow Designer.
