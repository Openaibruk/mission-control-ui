# Standard Operating Procedure: Refunds & Returns

**Document ID:** CHIP-FIN-001
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-03-16
**Owner:** Finance & Customer Success Teams
**Review Cycle:** Quarterly

---

## 1. Purpose

To provide clear, fair, and consistent guidelines for processing customer refunds, returns, and credits while protecting ChipChip's financial health and maintaining customer trust.

## 2. Scope

All refund, return, credit, and compensation transactions across all product categories and order types.

## 3. Refund Eligibility

### 3.1 Eligible for Full Refund

- Product quality issue (spoilage, damage, contamination) reported within **24 hours** with photo evidence
- Wrong item delivered
- Missing item(s) from order
- Order not delivered (confirmed lost)
- Delivery outside promised window by more than **4 hours** (customer may choose refund or credit)
- Product significantly different from listing description

### 3.2 Eligible for Partial Refund

- Minor quality issue (cosmetic damage, slight ripeness variance) — refund for affected items only
- Order partially fulfilled — refund for unavailable items
- Weight-based items that are **>15% under** stated weight — refund proportional to shortage

### 3.3 Not Eligible for Refund

- Change of mind after delivery (unless within returnable categories below)
- Quality issue reported **after 24-hour window** (exception: health/safety concerns)
- Customer provided incorrect delivery address
- Items consumed or partially consumed before reporting issue
- Failure to be available for delivery (after retry attempt)

### 3.4 Returnable Categories

Most perishable items are **not returnable** due to food safety. Exceptions:

| Category | Returnable? | Conditions |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Fresh produce | No | Refund with photo evidence only |
| Dairy/eggs | No | Refund with photo evidence only |
| Meat/seafood | No | Refund with photo evidence only |
| Packaged goods (sealed) | Yes | Within 7 days, unopened, with receipt |
| Specialty/dry goods | Yes | Within 7 days, unopened, with receipt |
| Household items | Yes | Within 14 days, unused, original packaging |

## 4. Refund Methods

| Method | When Used | Processing Time |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| Original payment method | Default for all refunds | 5-10 business days |
| ChipChip Credit | Customer preference or small amounts (<$10) | Instant |
| Store credit + bonus | Retention-focused (offer 110% value as credit) | Instant |
| Bank transfer | When original payment method unavailable | 3-5 business days |
| Cash (in-person) | Only for COD orders, with manager approval | Same day |

**Important:** Always offer ChipChip credit as a first option (with the 110% bonus incentive). Customers who accept credit are 3x more likely to reorder.

## 5. Processing Procedure

### Step 1: Request Received

1. Customer submits refund request via app, email, phone, or chat.
2. Agent creates a **Refund Ticket** with:
   - Customer ID and order number
   - Reason for refund (use standard categories)
   - Supporting evidence (photos, screenshots)
   - Requested refund method

### Step 2: Validation

1. Verify the order exists and belongs to the customer.
2. Confirm the issue is within the reporting window.
3. Check the customer's refund history — flag if >3 refunds in 30 days (potential abuse).
4. Review evidence provided.

### Step 3: Approval

| Refund Amount | Approval Required |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Up to $15 | Auto-approved (CS Agent) |
| $15.01 - $50 | CS Team Lead |
| $50.01 - $150 | Customer Success Manager |
| $150.01 - $500 | Operations Manager |
| Over $500 | Director of Operations |

**Fraud flag:** If customer has >3 refunds in 30 days, all refunds require CS Team Lead approval regardless of amount.

### Step 4: Execution

1. Process refund in the payment system or issue credit.
2. Send confirmation to customer:
   > "Your refund of $[X] has been processed. [If original payment: It will appear in your account within 5-10 business days. / If credit: $[X] has been added to your ChipChip wallet.] Ticket #[ID] for your reference."
3. If product quality issue: file supplier incident report.
4. If delivery issue: flag for logistics review.

### Step 5: Record Keeping

- Log all refunds in the Finance tracking system.
- Tag with reason code, category, and approval level.
- Monthly reconciliation against payment processor records.

## 6. Credit & Compensation Guidelines

### ChipChip Credit (Preferred)

| Scenario | Credit Amount |
|----------|--------------|
| Minor inconvenience | $5 |
| Product quality issue | Value of affected items + $5 |
| Late delivery (2-4 hours) | Delivery fee refund + $5 |
| Late delivery (>4 hours) | Full order credit + $10 |
| Significant service failure | Full order credit + $15-25 |
| Retention gesture (at-risk customer) | $10-25 (discretionary) |

### Credit Expiry

- Standard credits: **90 days** from issue date
- Promotional credits: as stated in promotion terms
- Loyalty rewards credits: **180 days** from issue date
- Send expiry reminder **7 days** before credit expires

## 7. Dispute Resolution

### Customer Disagrees with Decision

1. **First escalation:** CS Team Lead reviews — can overturn agent decisions.
2. **Second escalation:** Customer Success Manager reviews — can approve up to $150.
3. **Final escalation:** Operations Manager — final decision.

At each level: review the case fresh, don't just rubber-stamp the previous decision.

### Chargeback / Payment Dispute

1. Finance team is notified immediately of any chargeback.
2. Gather all evidence: order confirmation, delivery proof, communication logs.
3. Submit dispute response to payment processor within **3 business days**.
4. Customer account flagged — future orders may require prepayment.
5. If chargeback is won: account flag removed.
6. If chargeback is lost: log as loss, review process for improvement.

## 8. Reporting

### Monthly Refund Report
- Total refunds issued ($ amount and count)
- Refunds by category (quality, missing, late, etc.)
- Refunds by product type
- Refund rate (% of total revenue)
- Chargeback rate
- Top refund reasons (actionable insights)
- Supplier-attributed refunds (for supplier scorecard)

### Thresholds for Review

| Metric | Yellow Flag | Red Flag |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Monthly refund rate | > 3% of revenue | > 5% of revenue |
| Chargeback rate | > 0.5% of transactions | > 1% of transactions |
| Repeat refund customers | > 5% of refund customers | > 10% of refund customers |
| Single supplier refund rate | > 5% of their orders | > 10% of their orders |
