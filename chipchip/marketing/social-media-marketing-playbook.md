## 1. Brand Voice & Personality

### Brand Voice Attributes

| Attribute | We Are | We Are Not |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Tone | Friendly, casual, energetic | Corporate, stiff, salesy |
| Language | Simple, everyday words | Jargon-heavy, overly formal |
| Attitude | Helpful neighbor, community-first | Distant corporation |
| Humor | Light, relatable, food-pun friendly | Sarcastic, edgy, offensive |

### Key Phrases to Use
- "Fresh from [farm/supplier name] to your door"
- "Save more when you buy together"
- "Your neighborhood's group buy"
- "Quality you can taste, prices you'll love"

### Phrases to Avoid
- "Cheapest prices" (we compete on value, not just price)
- "Cut out the middleman" (we ARE the platform — be honest about our role)
- Any claims about health benefits we can't substantiate

## 2. Platform Strategy

### Instagram
- **Posting frequency:** 5-7x/week (feed), daily Stories, 2-3 Reels/week
- **Content mix:**
  - 40% — Product showcases (beautiful food photography)
  - 25% — Customer stories and testimonials
  - 20% — Behind-the-scenes (supplier visits, warehouse, team)
  - 10% — Educational (recipes, storage tips, seasonal guides)
  - 5% — Promotional (deals, new launches)
- **Hashtag strategy:** #ChipChip #FreshToDoor #GroupBuy #FarmFresh [local food hashtags]

### Facebook
- **Posting frequency:** 4-5x/week
- **Content mix:** Similar to Instagram, longer captions, community-focused
- **Facebook Groups:** Create neighborhood-specific groups for group buy coordination
- **Events:** Promote seasonal group buys as Facebook Events

### TikTok
- **Posting frequency:** 3-5x/week
- **Content types:**
  - "Day in the life of a ChipChip order" (unboxing, cooking)
  - Supplier spotlight videos
  - Price comparison challenges ("$20 grocery haul")
  - Recipe hacks using ChipChip products
  - Trending audio + food content

### Twitter/X
- **Posting frequency:** 1-2x/day
- **Use for:** Customer service, real-time updates, industry commentary, witty engagement

### WhatsApp/Telegram
- **Use for:** Group buy coordination, delivery updates, flash deals
- **Frequency:** As needed — don't spam. Max 3 broadcast messages/week.

## 3. Content Calendar Framework

### Weekly Rhythm

| Day | Theme | Content Type |
|-----|-------|-------------|
| Monday | Fresh Start | New product highlights, weekly deals announcement |
| Tuesday | Tips Tuesday | Storage tips, recipe ideas, food facts |
| Wednesday | Supplier Spotlight | Story about a farm/vendor, photos/video |
| Thursday | Community Day | Customer stories, group buy shoutouts, UGC reposts |
| Friday | Fresh Finds | Weekend specials, new arrivals, seasonal picks |
| Saturday | Fun/Engagement | Polls, quizzes, contests, behind-the-scenes |
| Sunday | Meal Prep | Weekly meal planning content, batch cooking ideas |

### Monthly Campaigns

- **Week 1:** New supplier launch campaign
- **Week 2:** Themed sale (e.g., "Tropical Fruit Week," "Dairy Deals")
- **Week 3:** Community spotlight + referral push
- **Week 4:** End-of-month summary + teaser for next month

## 4. User-Generated Content (UGC) Strategy

1. **Encourage sharing:** Include a card in every delivery asking customers to share their unboxing/meal with #ChipChipFinds.
2. **Weekly UGC contest:** Best photo wins $20 credit — repost all entries.
3. **Permission protocol:** Always ask before reposting. DM template:
   > "Love this photo! 😍 Would you mind if we shared it on our page? We'll credit you, of course!"

## 5. Influencer & Partnership Guidelines

### Micro-Influencer Program (1K-50K followers)
- **Offer:** Free monthly ChipChip credit ($50-100) + commission on referral codes
- **Requirements:** 2 feed posts + 4 Stories per month, must disclose partnership
- **Selection criteria:** Local food bloggers, home cooks, community figures
- **Onboarding:** Send brand guide, content examples, unique referral code

### Macro-Influencer (50K+ followers)
- Requires Marketing Manager approval
- Negotiated terms per campaign
- Must include measurable KPIs (reach, conversions)

## 6. Paid Social Advertising

### Budget Allocation (Monthly)
| Channel | % of Social Ad Budget |
|---------|---------------------|
| Instagram/Facebook | 50% |
| TikTok | 30% |
| Google/YouTube | 15% |
| Other (Twitter, Pinterest) | 5% |

### Target Audiences
1. **Primary:** Age 25-45, urban/suburban, grocery shoppers, health-conscious
2. **Secondary:** Parents with young children, meal preppers
3. **Tertiary:** Budget-conscious households, community organizers

### Ad Types & Objectives
| Objective | Format | Target CPA |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Awareness | Video (15-30s), carousel | N/A (optimize for reach) |
| Consideration | Testimonial ads, comparison ads | $3-5 per click |
| Conversion | Dynamic product ads, promo code ads | $8-15 per first order |
| Retargeting | Carousel of viewed items | $5-10 per re-engagement |

## 7. Metrics & Reporting

### Weekly Dashboard
- Follower growth per platform
- Engagement rate (likes + comments + shares / followers)
- Click-through rate to app/website
- UGC submissions
- Referral code redemptions

### Monthly Report
- Total reach and impressions
- Conversion rate from social traffic
- Cost per acquisition (paid campaigns)
- Top-performing content (by engagement and by conversions)
- Sentiment analysis summary
- Competitor benchmarking

## 8. Crisis Communication Protocol

1. **Identify:** Any post/comment going viral for negative reasons, or brand mention in negative context.
2. **Pause:** Immediately pause all scheduled promotional content.
3. **Assess:** Marketing Manager + PR lead evaluate severity within 30 minutes.
4. **Respond:** Public acknowledgment within 2 hours. Detailed resolution within 24 hours.
5. **Document:** Full incident report within 48 hours.
