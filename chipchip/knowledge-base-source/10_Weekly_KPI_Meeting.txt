📘 ChipChip Weekly KPI Meeting — 
1-Pager SOP
Meeting: Weekly KPI Review
Day: Friday
Total Duration: 2 Hours
Owner: Chief of Staff
Purpose: Enforce decisions, protect CP1/CP2, stop KPI drift
________________


🎯 Core Principle (Non-Negotiable)
KPI meetings are for decisions, not explanations.
 Explanations happen before executives join.
________________


🧱 Meeting Structure (3 Blocks)
🔹 Block 1 — Operator KPI Review 
Duration: 60 min
Participants: Team Leads only (Finance, Ops, Supply, Growth/B2B, Marketing, Tech)
Executives: ❌ Not allowed
Objective:
* Validate data

* Identify 🔴 KPIs

* Prepare exec-ready decisions







Flow:
   1. KPI Health Scan (20 min)

      * Each KPI labeled 🟢 / 🟡 / 🔴

      * Max 60 seconds per KPI

         2. Red KPI Resolution (30 min)
 For each 🔴 KPI:

            * Root cause (1 sentence)

            * Proposed action

            * Owner

            * Expected impact next week

               3. Exec Pack Prep (10 min)

                  * Max 8 slides

                  * Only 🔴 KPIs + decisions needed

Rules:
                     * ✅ Finance validates numbers live

























________________


🔹 Block 2 — Executive KPI Review
Duration: 45 min
Participants: Executives + Finance + relevant Team Leads only
Objective:
 Make decisions, apply vetoes, freeze actions.
Flow:
                        1. Executive Snapshot (10 min)

                           * CP1

                           * CP2

                           * Cash position


                              2. Red KPI Decisions (25 min)
 For each 🔴 KPI:

                                 * Owner states problem + proposal

                                 * Executives decide:

                                    * ✅ Approve

                                    * ❌ Block

                                    * 🔁 Modify

                                       3. Veto & Freeze (10 min)
 Executives may:

                                          * Freeze channels

                                          * Block discounts

                                          * Stop routes

                                          * Pause spend

                                          * Enforce automation

________________


🔹 Block 3 — Decision Lock
Duration: 15 min
Participants: Executives + CoS
Output (MANDATORY):

- Sent within 30 minutes after meeting

                                             * Becomes next week’s follow-up list

________________


🚦 Status Rules (Company-Wide)
                                                * 🟢 = On target / improving

                                                * 🟡 = Off target, corrective action active

                                                * 🔴 = Off target, no credible fix

🔴 KPI for 2 consecutive weeks → automatic escalation
________________


❌ What Is NOT Allowed
                                                   * Open-ended discussions

                                                   * KPIs without owners

________________


✅ Success Criteria
A good KPI meeting ends with:
                                                      * Fewer KPIs discussed next week

                                                      * Clear blocks & freezes applied

                                                      * Executives spending <45 minutes

                                                      * No “we’ll follow up later”

________________


🧠 Final Rule
If the same KPI appears red for 3 weeks, the system not the team has failed.
