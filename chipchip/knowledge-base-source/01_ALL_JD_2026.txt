SECTION 1: EXECUTIVE LEADERSHIP
________________


1. Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
Reports To:
Board / Founders
Mission:
Drive ChipChip to CP2 breakeven and scale sustainably through structural discipline, not aggressive growth.
Core Responsibilities:
* Define and protect company-level strategy

* Enforce RUN / IMPROVE / ENABLE separation

* Chair weekly CP1 / CP2 performance review

* Resolve cross-functional conflicts

* Approve new strategic initiatives

* Protect culture and execution rhythm

KPIs:
   * CP2 (Contribution Profit 2)

   * Cash runway

   * B2B share of volume

   * Cost per kg trend

Authority:
      * Freeze growth

      * Stop unprofitable routes

      * Override incentives

      * Reassign leadership responsibility

Cultural Standard:
Leads by discipline. Pays double in accountability. No tolerance for ambiguity.
