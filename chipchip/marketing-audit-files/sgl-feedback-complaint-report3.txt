SGL Feedback & Complaint Summary Report
Period: March 5 – March 10
Source: Telegram messages from SGL
1. Positive Feedback
Some SGL appreciated the quality of certain products:
* Potato quality was reported as very good.
* Carrot quality was also good in some deliveries.
* Some customers appreciated the 24-hour special discount orders.
________________


2. Product Quality Issues
Several concerns were raised regarding product quality:
* Onion quality reported as very small and dusty, some orders were returned.
* Beetroot quality reported as not as good as carrot.
* Inconsistent quality between different deliveries.
________________


3. Weight Shortage Complaints
Repeated complaints about missing weight (0.5 – 1 kg shortage).
Example:
* Potato shortage: 1 kg
* Tomato shortage: 0.5 kg
* The driver confirmed the issue during weighing.
* Customer reported to call center but did not receive feedback.
________________


4. Pricing Concerns
Some SGL reported that prices are increasing:
* Potato price increasing
* Onion price increasing
* Tomato mentioned around 24 birr in some markets
Customers requested:
* Better pricing
* Discount for large quantity orders (100 kg).
________________


5. Product Availability Issues
SGL reported out-of-stock problems for some products:
Examples:
* Soap orders could not be fulfilled due to stock shortage.
* Cabbage could not be ordered because ordering time closed.
Customers suggested adding more products such as:
* Pasta
* Macaroni
* Vinegar
* Ketchup
* Soap
________________


6. Delivery Requests
Some SGL requested 24-hour delivery for high-demand products:
Main requested items:
* Potato
* Onion
* Carrot
________________


7. Packaging Concern
One SGL mentioned that products delivered in boxes while fertilizer fee was charged, and asked whether the charge is for fertilizer or box rental.
________________


8. Company Responses
The company responded to customers by:
* Appreciating customer feedback
* Promising quality improvement
* Informing about new onion stock arriving
* Explaining discount limitations due to stock
* Confirming weight shortage investigation
________________


✅ Conclusion
Main improvement areas identified:
1. Product quality consistency
2. Weight accuracy during delivery
3. Better stock availability
4. Faster response from call center
5. Review of pricing and bulk order discounts
6. Consider adding additional grocery items
