What is a Super Leader?
A Super Leader is a shop owner who collects orders for their neighbors.
They are your "Anchor" on the route.
Instead of delivering to 50 individual houses, you make one single, large drop-off to the Super Leader. They handle the rest.
Goal: Build a network of ChipChip Pickup spots!
