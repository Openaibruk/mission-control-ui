Drivers Training Guideline 
Pick-Up Hub Project
Training Location: Warehouse
Responsible: Operation Manager & Logistics Manager

Training Topics / Guideline
1. Pick-Up Hub Delivery Process

   * Understand the Pick-Up Hub model and delivery flow.
   * Deliver products from the warehouse to assigned pickup hubs.

      2. Route & Delivery Planning

         * Follow assigned delivery routes and schedules.
         * Manage multiple hubs within one route efficiently.

            3. Product Handling

               * Handle fresh products carefully (vegetables, fruits).
               * Avoid damage during loading, transport, and unloading.

                  4. Packaging Handling

                     * Deliver bulk crates or packed products correctly.

                        5. Area Orders Handling

                           * Understand how area orders are grouped.
                           * Deliver the correct total kg to the pickup leader.

                              6. Quantity Verification

                                 * Check total quantity before leaving the warehouse.
                                 * Confirm quantity again with pickup leader during delivery.

                                    7. Quality Responsibility

                                       * Do not deliver visibly damaged products.
                                       * Report quality issues immediately to the warehouse team.


                                          8. Return Handling

                                             * Understand the process for returned or rejected products.
                                             * Record returns and bring them back to the warehouse.

                                                9. Communication with Pick-Up Leaders

                                                   * Inform pickup leaders before arrival.
                                                   * Confirm product handover clearly.

                                                      10. Delivery Documentation

                                                         * Follow delivery sheets or order documents.
                                                         * Ensure confirmation from the pickup leader after delivery.

Training Outcome:
 Drivers understand the pickup hub delivery system, handle products properly, manage routes efficiently, and coordinate smoothly with pickup leaders.
