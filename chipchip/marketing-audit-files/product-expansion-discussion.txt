Product Expansion Discussion
This report summarizes the product expansion brainstorming discussion from yesterday’s meeting. The goal was to identify which new products should be added to the app based on customer demand, field observation, and internal suggestions. The focus is on increasing sales, improving customer satisfaction, and maintaining strong inventory stability.




   Overview
