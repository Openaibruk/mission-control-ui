Pick Up leaders Training Guidline
Pick-Up Hub Leaders Training 
Preparation Guideline
Training Location: ChipChip Warehouse
Trainers: Operations Manager, Warehouse Manager, Quality Team
1. Purpose of the Training
* Understand how the Pick-Up Hub system works
* Learn how to handle products correctly
* Learn packing, quality checking, and order distribution
* Understand responsibilities between Warehouse, Logistics, and Pick-Up Leaders

2. Things Pick-Up Leaders Should Prepare
   * Bring notebook and pen for notes
   * Prepare questions about product handling or delivery process
   * Bring sample bags or packaging materials they normally use (if available)
   * Be ready to observe and practice during warehouse demonstrations

3. Training Topics
A. Product Types & Handling
      * Fresh products (vegetables, fruits) – sensitive items
      * FMCG products – packaged items
      * High-risk / easily damaged products
      * How to separate and store different product types
B. Quality Check
      * How to check product freshness and condition
      * Identifying damaged or low-quality items
      * What to do if quality problems are found

C. Packaging Process
         * With packaging option (individual packing)
         * Without packaging option (bulk delivery)
         * Proper use of bags, crates, and handling techniques

D. Order Receiving Process
            * How to receive products from drivers
            * How to verify quantity and product list
            * Signing delivery confirmation
E. Distribution to Members
            * Organizing member pickups
            * Managing group orders efficiently
            * Communication with group members
F. Handling Area Orders
            * Area orders will be communicated in advance (total KG shared earlier)
            * Warehouse will prepare the total order and drivers will deliver to the pick-up hub
            * Pick-Up Leader receives the full area order and manages distribution to members
            * Area order list may be shared as PDF or order summary
            * Pick-Up Leader organizes collection times for members
            * Ensure fair distribution and clear communication with group members
G. Returns & Issue Handling
            * What to do if products are damaged
            * Handling missing quantities
            * Reporting issues to the ChipChip team

4. Practical Demonstration
               * Real warehouse packing process
               * Product grading example
               * Correct crate and bag usage
               * Delivery handover simulation

5. Expected Outcome
After the training, Pick-Up Leaders should be able to:
                  * Properly receive and check products
                  * Handle and pack products correctly
                  * Manage member distribution smoothly
                  * Report issues clearly to the ChipChip team
