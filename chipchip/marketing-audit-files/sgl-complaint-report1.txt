📊 SGL Complaint Report
📅 Period: February 14 – February 22
1️⃣ Product Quality Issues (Most Frequent Complaints)
The majority of SGL complaints during this period were about vegetable quality, especially:
* 🥕 Carrot – Not fresh, not clean, poor appearance (images provided by SGLs).
* 🧅 Red Onion – Wet, very small size, poor quality.
* 🥔 Potato – Had growth/sprout parts, bad appearance, not attractive for customers.
* For example from Eyobe SG  Koy zenjebel nw wey eskebal deres keld nw ende yeyaznew yelakachut shenkurt yezekete demo weshetachu ere ene esun shenkurt 25 birr gezugn lamtalachu mn aynet sera nw meserut be shufer gulbet be nedaj be egna gulbet metashofut
📌 Impact: 
* SGLs reported customer dissatisfaction.
* Some SGLs said they are losing customers and profit.
* Some are scared to order these products again.
* Before ordering, they ask: “Should I order potatoes?”
* Repeated complaints are affecting confidence.
✅ Note: From February 21, potato quality has improved and is now reported as good.
2️⃣ Pricing Concerns
* One SGL mentioned 1kg beetroot is 31 birr in the garment market but 43 birr in ChipChip, creating price competition issues.
* Complaints about tomato commission decrease.Some SGLs are concerned about lower margins.
3️⃣ Return Request
* One SGL requested the return of Medabireya.


4️⃣ Stock Request
* SGLs requested that Kono Soap (Kono Samuna) be available in stock.
⚠️ Overall Situation
* These complaints are repeated frequently.
* Quality issues are directly affecting:
   * Customer retention
   * SGL trust
   * Order volume
   * Profitability
All SGLs are requesting a permanent solution, especially regarding vegetable quality control.
