Super Group Leader Awareness & Activation Campaign
________________


Campaign Objective
To create strong awareness, understanding, and emotional motivation around the Super Group Leader (SGL) Program, while driving sign-ups and increasing participation through content marketing and targeted SMS communication.
________________


Core Message
In-App & Campaign Theme:
“ጉርብትናዎን ይመንዝሩት”
(Empowering customers to earn by monetizing their network.)
________________


Campaign Structure & Content Strategy
Phase 1: Teaser Phase (Awareness Building)
Goal: Spark curiosity and prepare the audience for an upcoming opportunity.
Key Messaging:
* “Big things are coming”
* “Coming soon”
* “A new opportunity is on the way”
Channels:
* Social media teaser posts
* SMS (teaser messages only)
* Push notifications
Focus:
* Build anticipation without revealing full details
* Encourage curiosity and discussion
* Prepare the audience for the official reveal
________________


Phase 2: Reveal Phase (Education & Value Explanation)
Goal: Clearly communicate what the Super Group Leader Program is and how customers benefit.
Key Messaging:
* What the Super Group Leader Program is
* How it works (simple step-by-step explanation)
* Who can join and how to get started
* Key benefits and incentives
Content Focus:
* Program overview posts
* Explainer videos or graphics
* FAQ-style content
Expected Outcome:
* Strong understanding of the program
* Reduced confusion and hesitation
* Increased intent to join
________________


Phase 3: Emotional Connection Phase (Trust & Inspiration)
Goal: Build emotional trust and motivation through real success stories.
Key Messaging:
* “People like you are already benefiting”
* “This opportunity is changing lives”
Content Types:
* Interviews with current Super Group Leaders
* Testimonials and success stories
* Short documentary-style videos
Focus:
* Human stories over promotions
* Relatability and social proof
________________


SMS Target Segments
1. High-Value Active Customers (Conversion Target)
* Audience: 10,000 top customers active in the last 6 months
* Goal: Convert 2% (200 users) into Super Group Leaders
2. Low-Activity Super Group Leaders (Activation Target)
* Audience: Existing Super Group Leaders with less than 5 orders
* Goal: Increase order frequency and engagement
________________


SMS Campaign Plan
SMS Campaign Duration
Start Date: February 2
Duration: 1 Month
(This duration applies only to SMS communication.)
________________


SMS Target 1: Active Customers
* Objective: Convert active customers into Super Group Leaders
* Messaging Focus:
   * Super Group Leader benefits
   * Earning potential and incentives
   * Simple steps to join
   * Urgency-driven calls to action
________________


SMS Target 2: Low-Activity Super Group Leaders
* Objective: Encourage more frequent ordering
* Messaging Focus:
   * Performance-based rewards
   * Commission and free delivery benefits
   * Motivational reminders to stay active
________________


Content & Messaging Approach
1. Benefit-Driven Communication
* Clear breakdown of commissions, savings, and rewards
* Simple and direct language
* Strong CTAs to join or place orders
2. Life-Changing Opportunity Messaging
* Position SGL as a long-term income opportunity
* Emphasize empowerment, independence, and growth
* Highlight real success stories
________________


Overall Expected Impact
* Increased awareness and understanding of the Super Group Leader Program
* Higher emotional engagement and trust
* Improved SMS-driven conversions
* Increased activity among existing Super Group Leaders
* Stronger ChipChip community and loyalty
