SUPER GROUP LEADER (SGL)
Conversion & Acquisition Strategy


Strategy Objective
Shift ChipChip’s growth model from high-cost individual B2C orders to a group-based B2B2C (SGL) model that:

*  Reduces operational and logistics cost
* Increases order value and margin
* Creates income opportunities for community leaders
* Maintains or increases total order volume

Clear Targets (Numbers)

* Reduce B2C orders: ↓ 10% per quarter
* Grow B2B2C orders: ↑ 5% per cycle
* Primary growth engine: Super Group Leaders
* Target margin: 10%
* SGL commission: 5% net

Definition: Super Group Leader (SGL)

A Super Group Leader is:
   * One person ordering on behalf of minimum 10 people
   * No upper limit (30+ encouraged)
   * Receives one consolidated delivery
   * Manages:
   * Group collection
   * Order placement
   * Delivery coordination
Benefits to ChipChip
   * One delivery instead of 10+
   * Lower packaging and manpower cost
   * Higher order value per delivery
