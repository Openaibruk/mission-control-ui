📊 Survey Team Performance Improvement Strategy bruk@chipchip.social 
Objective:
 Increase survey productivity, accountability, and quality while shifting compensation to a performance-driven structure.
________________


1️⃣ Performance Targets
🎯 Daily Target
* Minimum: 40 completed calls per agent per day

🎯 Weekly Target
   * 40 calls × 6 days = 240 calls per week per agent

🎯 Monthly Expectation
      * 950+ calls per agent per month (average)

This ensures:
         * Higher output

         * Predictable survey completion timeline

         * Strong performance culture

________________


2️⃣ New Compensation Structure (Balanced & Motivating)
💰 Base Salary
2,500 birr per month (Fixed)
This keeps stability but shifts focus toward performance.
________________


💸 Performance Incentive (Tier System)
Calls Completed (Monthly)
	Incentive Per Call
	0 – 200 calls
	3 birr per call
	201 – 240 calls
	4 birr per call
	241+ calls
	5 birr per call
	Why this works:
            * Encourages agents to push beyond minimum

            * High performers earn more than before

            * Low performers naturally earn less

________________


3️⃣ Weekly Performance Bonus
To increase competition:
🥇 Weekly Top Performer Bonus
               * Highest completed calls (with quality approval)

               * Bonus: 300 birr

Conditions:
                  * Must meet minimum 250 weekly target

                  * Quality check must pass

This creates urgency and weekly motivation.
________________


4️⃣ Quality Control System
To prevent fake calls or rushed surveys:
✔ Randomly verify 5–10% of completed calls
✔ Monitor average call duration
✔ Review response accuracy
✔ Incomplete or fake calls are not counted
Performance without quality will not be rewarded.
________________


5️⃣ Daily Monitoring System
Create a shared performance sheet including:
                     * Calls attempted

                     * Calls completed

                     * Rejected calls

                     * Average call duration

                     * Daily total

                     * Weekly total

📌 Leaderboard visible to all agents.
Transparency increases accountability.
________________


6️⃣ Performance Review System
Monthly Evaluation:
Each agent evaluated based on:
                        * Total calls completed

                        * Target achievement rate

                        * Quality score

                        * Consistency (daily performance)

If agent fails to reach:
                           * 200 calls for 2 consecutive months → warning

                           * Continued underperformance → replacement review

________________


7️⃣ Implementation Plan
Week 1:
                              * Announce new structure

                              * Explain earning potential

                              * Clarify quality standards

                              * Begin daily tracking

Month 1:
                                 * Monitor adjustment

                                 * Give weekly bonus

                                 * Collect feedback

Month 2:
                                    * Evaluate results

                                    * Adjust targets if necessary

________________


8️⃣ Communication Strategy (Very Important)
Position it positively:
Instead of:
“We are reducing base salary.”
Say:
“We are upgrading the system so high performers can earn more and grow faster. Your income will now depend on your effort and results.”
Frame it as:
                                       * Growth opportunity

                                       * Performance recognition

                                       * Fair system

________________


📈 Expected Results
✔ 30–50% increase in call volume
✔ Higher accountability
✔ Reduced laziness
✔ Stronger competitive environment
✔ Cost efficiency for company
