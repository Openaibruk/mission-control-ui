Packaging Preference Feature (structure & Survey)

Project Overview
This project introduces a Packaging Preference option (With / Without packaging) in our ordering and delivery process. The goal is to reduce plastic bag usage, give customers choice, and test operational feasibility through a survey before full rollout.
Objectives
* Reduce single-use plastic bags
* Give customers flexibility and cost transparency
* Test customer willingness to choose no-packaging options
* Understand operational impact (delivery, madaberiya, timing)
* Collect data to support product and policy decisions
Scope
* Applies to selected products and locations (pilot phase)
* Covers delivery and madaberiya (pickup point) options
* Includes cost, timing, and quantity handling














Feature Structure
A. Packaging Preference Option (at checkout)
Customers must choose one option:
Option 1: With Packaging
* Packaging cost added to order to all members 25 birr each 
* Standard packaging (plastic or eco, based on availability)
* Normal delivery process
* No change to delivery time
Option 2: Without Packaging
* Only the group leader will pay 25 birr 
* Product delivered without plastic packaging
* Delivery options:
   * 1 pack (pickup point)  no Splitting 
   * Direct delivery (only where feasible)
* Check buffer time (add only if necessary)
* Full quantity delivered as is


Operational Considerations
* Product suitability (dry vs liquid, fragile vs non-fragile)
* Hygiene and quality control
* Delivery partner awareness and training
* Clear labeling: “No Packaging – Customer Accepted”
* Customer responsibility acknowledgment


Risks & Mitigation
* Risk: Product damage → Limit to suitable products
* Risk: Delivery confusion → Clear system tags & training
* Risk: Customer complaints → Clear explanation before checkout




Survey Guideline – Packaging Preference Feature
* This survey is about the Packaging Preference option (With / Without packaging) that is being implemented.
* The feature will go live, this survey is to check if the option is clear, acceptable, and workable for your role.
* Please answer based on your real work experience, not assumptions.
* There are no right or wrong answers, honest feedback is important.
* If something is unclear or difficult, mention it so we can fix it early.
* Focus on operations, delivery, quality, and customer experience.
* Use the comment sections to share specific issues or suggestions.
* Your feedback will be used to improve execution, not to evaluate performance.

Key Points to Tell Customers
* They have a choice: At checkout, they can pick either:

   * With Cotton Bag (15 Birr) – reusable, eco-friendly

   * Without Packaging (Free) – delivered directly without splitting crates

      * Why this matters:

         * Promotes sustainability and reduces plastic waste

         * Gives flexibility depending on their needs

            * How it works:

               * First order: encourage buying the 15 Birr reusable bag to introduce the concept

               * Next orders: they can freely choose free packaging since the cotton bag is reusable

                  * No pressure: Buying the cotton bag is optional, not mandatory

                  * Safety & convenience: Products will still be delivered safely, whether packaged or not
