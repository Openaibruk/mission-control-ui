STRATEGY FOR CURRENT SUPER GROUP LEADERS

 Objective
* Retain existing SGL
* Increase their volume & earnings
* Turn them into ambassadors for new SGL
* Avoid confusion or feeling “replaced”



SEGMENT CURRENT SGL (FIRST STEP)
Split them into 3 groups:
A. Active & High-Volume SGL
   * Order regularly
   * Handle groups well

Treatment
      * Priority support
      * Early access to new features
      * Featured in content & testimonials
      * First candidates for Level-up SGL

________________


B. Active but Low-Volume SGL
         * Order, but small groups or irregular
Treatment
         * Education:
         * How to earn more
         * Product mix guidance

            * Sales support:
            * Help them grow group size
            * Motivation messages (earnings focus)

________________


C. Inactive / Dormant SGL
               * Signed up but not ordering
Treatment
               * Re-activation campaign
               * Simple restart message:
               * “Let’s help you place your first group order”
               * One-on-one support call

________________


COMMUNICATION STRATEGY (CURRENT SGL)
Messaging Tone
                  * “You are already a Group Leader”
                  * “You are part of our core group”
                  * “We are building this together”

Channels
                     * Direct SMS
                     * Calls from Sales / Ops

________________





INCREASE THEIR EARNINGS FIRST
Before asking them to recruit others:
                        * Educate them on:
                        * Higher-margin products
                        * Selling bundles

                           * Show:
                           * Earnings comparison (5 birr vs 15 birr)

                              * Help them:
                              * Expand to neighbors
                              * Increase group size

________________


TURN THEM INTO AMBASSADORS
Once stable:
                                 * Ask them to:
                                 * Refer new SUPs
                                 * Appear in testimonials
                                 * Join “Day with SUP” content

Incentive:
                                    * Recognition
                                    * Small bonuses
                                    * Visibility

________________






DO NOT CHANGE RULES SUDDENLY
If you introduce:
                                       * Website onboarding
                                       * Levels
                                       * New processes

Then:
                                          * Auto-assign them to Level 1 or Level-up path
                                          * Explain changes clearly

________________


KPIs FOR CURRENT SUPs
                                             * Retention rate
                                             * Orders per SUP
                                             * Group size growth
                                             * Upgrade rate to Level-up SUP
