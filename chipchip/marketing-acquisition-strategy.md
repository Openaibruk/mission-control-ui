# ChipChip Customer Acquisition Strategy

**Prepared by:** Nova + Nahom (Marketing)  
**Date:** March 18, 2026

---

## Executive Summary

ChipChip's growth depends on three acquisition engines: **Super Group Leaders (SGLs)** who aggregate last-mile demand, **Restaurants** who provide recurring B2B volume, and **Direct Customers** who drive mass adoption. This strategy provides a 90-day action plan to accelerate all three.

**Current state:** 83,731 total orders, 153,989 kg monthly volume, 7.4M ETB monthly sales.

---

## 1. The Acquisition Funnel

   ↓           ↓         ↓          ↓            ↓          ↓

## 2. SGL (Super Group Leader) Acquisition

SGLs are the most valuable acquisition channel — each one aggregates 10-50 households.

### Current SGL Funnel
1. **Awareness:** Driver mentions, word of mouth, occasional social media
2. **Interest:** Understand the 5% commission model
3. **Signup:** Google Form or app registration
4. **Activation:** First group order placed
5. **Retention:** Weekly ordering habit

### Growth Strategies

#### A. Driver-Led Recruitment (Highest ROI)
Drivers see SGL potential in their routes.

- **Milestone incentives:** Driver recruits 3 SGLs → bonus. 5 SGLs → bigger bonus. 10 SGLs → vehicle upgrade support
- **Recognition:** "Top Recruiter" monthly award on driver group
- **Training:** 15-min pitch card for drivers to explain SGL program
- **Target:** 10 new SGLs/month per active driver (currently ~3-5)

#### B. Community Mapping (Systematic)
Identify areas without SGL coverage.

- **Data-driven targeting:** Map order density by area → find clusters without an SGL
- **"Neighborhood Captain" campaign:** Targeted social media posts for underserved areas
- **Community leaders:** Partner with kebele leaders, church groups, women's groups
- **Target:** 5 new SGLs/month from mapped areas

#### C. SGL Referral Program
Existing SGLs recruit new SGLs.

- **Bonus:** 500 ETB for each referred SGL who completes first 5 orders
- **Recognition:** "Network Builder" badge on profile
- **Target:** 3 referrals/SGL/year

#### D. Event-Based Recruitment
- Market days: Set up booths at Merkato and local markets
- Community events: Sponsor local events, pitch SGL program
- University campuses: Student SGLs for campus communities

### SGL Activation (Post-Signup)

**The first 7 days are critical.** Most SGL drop-off happens here.

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Day 0 | Welcome message + quick-start guide (in app) |
| Day 1 | Personal call from marketing team |
| Day 2 | Share sample group order template |
| Day 3 | Provide printable flyers for their neighborhood |
| Day 5 | Check-in: "How's it going? Need help?" |
| Day 7 | First order incentive (extra 2% commission) |
| Day 14 | Milestone celebration (if active) |

### SGL Retention
- Weekly order reminders
- Monthly commission reports (show them what they've earned)
- Quarterly SGL meetups and networking
- Performance-based tier system (Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum)

## 3. Restaurant Acquisition

### Restaurant Funnel
1. **Awareness:** Social media, industry events, SGL referrals
2. **Interest:** Understand reliability, BNPL, bulk pricing
3. **Trial:** First order experience
4. **Contract:** Recurring order agreement
5. **Expansion:** Increase order volume

### Growth Strategies

#### A. Restaurant Walk-Outs (Direct Sales)
- Target restaurants in known ChipChip delivery zones
- Pitch: reliable supply, next-morning delivery, flexible credit (BNPL)
- Sample order: First order at 20% discount
- Target: 5 new restaurants/month

#### B. SGL → Restaurant Pipeline
SGLs know restaurant owners in their communities.

- Referral bonus: 1,000 ETB for successful restaurant signup
- Monthly "referrer of the month" recognition

#### C. Restaurant Association Partnerships
- Partner with Addis Ababa Restaurant Association
- Sponsor association events
- Offer association members exclusive terms

### Restaurant Retention
- Dedicated account manager for first 90 days
- Weekly order confirmations
- Monthly business review (share data insights)
- BNPL credit limit increases based on loyalty

- **Lookalike audiences:** Build from existing customer data
- **Monthly budget:** 5,000 ETB for paid boosts

- **Give 15%, Get 15%:** Referral discount for both parties
- **Track via unique referral codes** in app

- Partner with apartment complexes and condominiums
- Offer "Building Launch" — first 50 orders from a building get free delivery
- Resident committee endorsement

## 5. Geographic Expansion Strategy

### Phase 1: Consolidate Addis Ababa (Month 1-3)
- Fill SGL coverage gaps in existing delivery zones
- Increase order density in current areas (lower per-order cost)
- Focus: Kirkos, Addis Ketema, Arada, Bole, Kirkos

### Phase 2: Expand Addis Periphery (Month 4-6)
- Add new sub-cities: Akaky Kaliti, Nifas Silk, Kolfe Keranio
- Deploy SGL-first strategy: recruit SGLs before marketing to customers
- Target: 2 new sub-cities/quarter

### Phase 3: Secondary Cities (Month 7-12)
- Hawassa, Adama, Bahir Dar
- Pilot with restaurant segment first (easier logistics)
- Then introduce SGL model

### Unit Economics to Track
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Total marketing spend ÷ new customers
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** Avg order value × order frequency × retention period
- **LTV:CAC ratio:** Target > 3:1
- **Payback period:** How many orders to recover CAC
