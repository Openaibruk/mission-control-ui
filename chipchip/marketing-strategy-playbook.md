## Executive Summary

ChipChip is Ethiopia's technology-first food supply chain platform — connecting farmers, suppliers, restaurants, and consumers through digitally orchestrated logistics. Monthly sales: 7.4M ETB, 83K+ orders, growing 20%+ MoM.

This playbook consolidates our entire marketing strategy into one document: who we are, who we serve, how we communicate, how we grow, and how we work efficiently. Every member of the marketing team should read this in their first week.

**Core message:** "We don't touch products. We control flows."

## Part 2: Who We Serve

### Customer Segment Matrix

| Segment | Volume Share | Revenue Share | Avg Order | Key Need |
|---------|-------------|---------------|-----------|----------|
| **Individual Consumers** | 70.9% | 72.4% | 2.14 kg / 88 ETB | Affordable, reliable delivery |
| **Super Leaders** | 15.5% | 11.6% | 30+ kg | Commission, community tools |
| **Restaurants/HORECA** | 11% | 15.8% | 100+ kg | Reliability, credit, bulk pricing |
| **B2B Captives** | 2.6% | 0.2% | Variable | Recurring orders, aggregation |

### Persona: Super Group Leader (SGL)
- **Who:** Community organizer, trusted by 20-50 households
- **Motivation:** Earn commission (5% GMV), help community save money
- **Pain:** Manual coordination, unreliable platforms
- **Goal:** Easy tools to aggregate and manage group orders

### Persona: Restaurant Owner
- **Who:** Small to medium restaurant in Addis Ababa
- **Motivation:** Reliable supply at predictable prices, flexible credit
- **Pain:** Price volatility, unreliable suppliers, cash flow strain
- **Goal:** Set-and-forget ordering with BNPL flexibility

### Persona: End Consumer
- **Who:** Urban household, price-sensitive, smartphone user
- **Motivation:** Save money on groceries, convenient delivery
- **Pain:** High delivery fees (150 ETB), unreliable supply
- **Goal:** Affordable, fresh groceries without hassle

## Part 4: Acquisition Strategy Summary

*Full document: `marketing-acquisition-strategy.md`*

### Three Growth Engines

#### 🚀 Engine 1: SGL Acquisition (Highest Value)
- **Target:** 15 → 25 → 35 new SGLs/month (Months 1-3)
- **Methods:** Driver recruitment, community mapping, SGL referrals, events
- **Critical:** First 7-day onboarding sequence (automated)
- **Budget:** 10,000 ETB/month

#### 🍽️ Engine 2: Restaurant Acquisition (Highest Revenue)
- **Target:** 5 → 8 → 10 new restaurants/month
- **Methods:** Walk-out sales, SGL referrals, association partnerships
- **Offer:** First order at 20% discount, BNPL terms
- **Budget:** 5,000 ETB/month

#### 📱 Engine 3: Direct Customer (Highest Volume)
- **Target:** 200 → 350 → 500 new signups/month
- **Methods:** Social content, referral program, community partnerships
- **CPA Target:** <100 ETB/customer
- **Budget:** 8,000 ETB/month

### Geographic Strategy
- **Month 1-3:** Consolidate Addis Ababa (fill coverage gaps)
- **Month 4-6:** Expand Addis periphery (new sub-cities)
- **Month 7-12:** Secondary cities (Hawassa, Adama, Bahir Dar)
